# Partnership Marketing

Turn a proposed relationship into an accountable shared-customer mechanism. Optimize incremental retained customer value above full joint cost and risk, not partner count, logos, announced reach, leads exchanged, content volume, attributed pipeline, or signed agreements alone.

Read [partnership-contract.md](references/partnership-contract.md) before accepting audience, access, contribution, rights, data, commercial, or result claims. Read [mechanisms-and-pilots.md](references/mechanisms-and-pilots.md) before choosing co-marketing, co-selling, channel, affiliate, integration, marketplace, or ecosystem mechanics. Read [measurement-and-governance.md](references/measurement-and-governance.md) before defining attribution, incentives, economics, operations, conflicts, renewal, or exit. Use [playbook-sources.md](references/playbook-sources.md) to cite the pinned Growth Playbook basis.

## Select One Mode

| Mode | Use |
| --- | --- |
| `assessment` | Determine whether a proposed partner and mechanism deserve validation, a pilot, defer, or rejection |
| `design` | Define a shared-value proposition, bounded mechanism, contribution contract, pilot, measurement, and handoff |
| `portfolio` | Audit, compare, govern, renew, renegotiate, pause, or exit several partnerships |

Name one primary mode, decision, internal owner, partner unit, customer unit, mechanism, horizon, investment boundary, and allowed action.

## Freeze The Partnership Contract

Record both parties and legal or operating entities; shared customer, user, buyer, payer, administrator, and beneficiary; customer job, current alternative, joint value, each party's independent value, prerequisites, exclusions, and failure conditions; product, offer, asset, channel, audience, account, access, contribution, capacity, service expectation, approval, brand, logo, trademark, content, proof, claim, confidentiality, data, lead, consent, suppression, identity, attribution, commercial terms, incentive, full cost, revenue or contribution share, billing, support, incident, term, renewal, termination, portability, customer communication, evidence, and requested external action.

Use `verified`, `inferred`, `unavailable`, or `not applicable` for evidence-bearing rows. A stakeholder or partner statement without inspectable support may be a `reported signal` with no evidence state. A public logo, announcement, directory listing, follower count, audience estimate, integration page, or memorandum does not verify permission, active delivery, qualified reach, customer ownership, economics, satisfaction, incrementality, or commitment.

## Require Shared-Customer Value

Map:

```text
qualified shared customer and job
-> each party's distinct contribution
-> combined experience or distribution path
-> customer first value and repeated value
-> attributable operating outcome
-> incremental retained contribution
-> renewal, expansion, or protected exit
```

Reject a partnership whose only rationale is brand association, executive preference, vague strategic value, database size, or access to another party's customers. Do not externalize spam, poor fit, support, privacy, brand, implementation, margin, or service cost to the customer or partner.

## Choose The Mechanism After Fit

Distinguish co-marketing, co-selling, referral, affiliate, reseller, distributor, integration, bundled offer, marketplace, event, content, data, and ecosystem relationships. One partnership may contain several mechanisms; give each separate customer value, eligibility, contribution, permission, attribution, economics, operations, and termination records.

Define who creates, approves, funds, distributes, receives, qualifies, follows up, delivers, supports, reports, corrects, and retires each element. Audience access never authorizes data transfer or contact. A co-branded asset requires scoped trademark, claim, content, derivative, channel, market, term, and approval rights.

Route customer referral mechanics to `referral-program-design`, reward design to `incentive-system-design`, acquisition portfolio decisions to `acquisition-strategy`, campaign assets to `campaign-planning`, sales assets and handoffs to `sales-enablement`, integrations and platform sourcing to `growth-infrastructure-assessment`, and causal tests to `experiment-design`.

## Pilot Before Dependence

Pilot the smallest customer, product, offer, channel, account, geography, and time boundary that can test the mechanism. Cap exposure, spend, leads, inventory, implementation, support, liability, and exclusivity as applicable. Define feasibility, qualified reach, first value, repeated value, economics, trust, operational quality, and counterfactual gates.

Specify positive, negative, harmful, and inconclusive actions before launch. Do not grant broad exclusivity, transfer strategic customer ownership, make irreversible integrations, or depend on one partner before verified marginal value, capacity, controls, portability, and exit work.

## Measure Without Double Credit

Keep partner-reported activity, operational attribution, jointly reconciled outcomes, association, prediction, and incrementality separate. Define party, entity, identity, eligibility, exposure, source, precedence, multi-partner overlap, customer value, cohort, window, maturity, currency, cost, and evidence.

Use full marginal economics: content and event production, media, affiliate or channel fees, discounts, incentives, revenue share, implementation, integration, tooling, support, sales, partner management, fraud, refunds, data, legal and policy operation, cannibalization, opportunity cost, and exit. Do not add both parties' attributed revenue or pipeline.

## Deliver In Order

Return:

1. mode, decision, owner, partner and customer unit, mechanism, horizon, and boundary;
2. evidence, relationship, permission, contribution, rights, data, commercial, and limitation ledger;
3. shared-customer value and mechanism map with alternatives and failure conditions;
4. partner-fit assessment or portfolio disposition;
5. contribution, asset, channel, lead, sales, delivery, support, and governance specification;
6. pilot with caps, owners, measurement, economics, decision rules, stop, rollback, and completion proof;
7. operating cadence, attribution and reconciliation, conflict, renewal, renegotiation, and exit plan;
8. specialist handoffs, Playbook sources, and unresolved evidence.

For China work, keep market, language, locale, partner entity, customer unit, platform, account, channel, contact and lead permission, data, hosting, transfer, content and brand review, advertising, contract, payment, tax, invoice, settlement, reseller or distributor role, exclusivity, support, and applicable rules separate. Do not infer WeChat, WeCom, Douyin, Xiaohongshu, another platform, partner access, permission, data use, payment, invoice, or legal condition from China or Simplified Chinese.

This Skill creates and reads local artifacts only. Do not discover, scrape, enrich, rank, contact, negotiate with, or message partners or customers; access partner, CRM, marketplace, social, ad, email, payment, contract, analytics, or product systems; exchange leads or data; use logos or private assets; create accounts; accept terms; sign agreements; grant rights or exclusivity; change prices, commissions, ownership, routing, data, integrations, or customer state; publish announcements or assets; launch pilots or campaigns; pay; invoice; deploy; or claim results.

## Completion Gate

Confirm that parties, customer, job, value, contributions, mechanism, eligibility, audience, access, permission, data, rights, claims, offer, commercial terms, attribution, economics, ownership, capacity, service, support, conflicts, concentration, pilot, measurement, renewal, exit, evidence, and action boundaries are explicit; logos are not value; audience is not permission; attributed pipeline is not incremental contribution; agreement is not operation; unavailable is not failure; and no external action occurred.
