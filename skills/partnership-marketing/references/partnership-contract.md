# Partnership Contract

## Intake

Capture independently:

- decision, internal owner, partner owner, approvers, mechanism, status, horizon, investment, review cadence, and allowed action;
- legal and operating party, product, market, language, locale, channel, account, platform, and route to market;
- shared customer, user, buyer, payer, administrator, implementation, support, and beneficiary units;
- customer problem, trigger, job, alternatives, first value, recurring value, prerequisites, exclusions, and trust;
- each party's product, audience, access, asset, expertise, service, distribution, data, funding, capacity, and operating contribution;
- joint value proposition, offer, path, content, event, campaign, integration, sales, delivery, and support experience;
- brand, logo, trademark, content, data, testimonial, proof, claim, confidentiality, derivative, distribution, market, term, and approval rights;
- audience definition, contact source, lead, account, identity, consent, suppression, sharing, purpose, access, retention, deletion, and incident;
- attribution, registration, precedence, overlap, ownership, routing, qualification, acceptance, handoff, follow-up, dispute, and customer control;
- fee, commission, discount, incentive, revenue or contribution share, currency, settlement, billing, refund, tax and invoice review, cost, payback, and liability;
- term, exclusivity, conflict, minimum, capacity, service level, exception, escalation, breach, renewal, renegotiation, termination, portability, and customer protection;
- source artifact, owner, date, evidence state, counterevidence, limitation, and freshness.

Version the contract when customer, mechanism, contribution, rights, data, commercial terms, attribution, service, market, or termination changes.

## Evidence Rules

Use `verified`, `inferred`, `unavailable`, or `not applicable`. Use `reported signal` for an attributable statement without inspectable support and leave evidence state blank.

Public evidence can verify visible announcements, partner pages, assets, product documentation, terms, directories, and published claims at a date. It cannot verify contract rights, current account access, qualified audience, lead permission, internal capacity, delivery, support, economics, satisfaction, incrementality, or exclusivity.

## Fit Record

Assess:

- shared-customer overlap and relevance;
- complementary rather than duplicative value;
- strategic and operating alignment;
- product, brand, trust, quality, and service compatibility;
- contribution credibility and capacity;
- data, rights, claims, and permission feasibility;
- channel, sales, implementation, support, and finance ownership;
- marginal economics, concentration, dependency, conflict, and reversibility;
- evidence time, downside, learning value, and exit.

Do not collapse fit into one score. A verified rights, data, customer-harm, economics, or exit blocker can outweigh broad brand fit.
