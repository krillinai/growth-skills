# Partnership Mechanisms And Pilots

## Mechanism Records

| Mechanism | Customer value gate | Distinct controls |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Co-marketing | Joint education or proof serves a qualified shared audience | brand, claims, asset rights, distribution, leads, consent, follow-up |
| Co-selling | Coordinated roles reduce buyer friction or improve solution fit | account ownership, registration, qualification, handoff, forecast, conflict |
| Referral | One party introduces a relevant customer who reaches value | sender-recipient value, permission, attribution, retained quality |
| Affiliate | Compensated promotion creates qualified retained customers | disclosure, media, traffic quality, commission, fraud, incrementality |
| Reseller or distributor | Partner sells or delivers with accountable customer support | territory, price, margin, contract, service, training, customer ownership |
| Integration | Combined products deliver a job neither path serves as well alone | product readiness, permissions, data, reliability, support, deprecation |
| Marketplace | A governed surface improves discovery, transaction, or fulfillment | listing, ranking, access, fees, identity, quality, disputes, dependency |
| Ecosystem or strategic | Several mechanisms reinforce durable customer value | portfolio ownership, governance, conflict, concentration, option value |

Do not use one metric or contract across incompatible mechanisms.

## Contribution Matrix

For every deliverable or workflow state record each party's input, source, owner, cost, due date, quality rule, rights, approval, capacity, dependency, output, receiver, exception, completion proof, and maintenance. Vague commitments such as promotion, support, leads, reach, or strategic access are not implementable contributions.

## Co-Marketing And Lead Handling

Define audience, customer question, position, claims, proof, asset, CTA, landing path, registration, organizer, brand and content rights, promotion plan, channel permission, attendee or lead consent, data controller or owner, sharing purpose, suppression, access, follow-up, deletion, and measurement.

An event registration or partner audience does not authorize every party to contact the person. Do not upload, exchange, enrich, or message lists as part of this Skill.

## Co-Selling And Channel Handling

Define account eligibility, customer relationship, registration, duplicate and existing-account rules, ownership, qualification, discovery, solution and commercial roles, price and discount authority, opportunity and forecast treatment, implementation, support, renewal, expansion, conflict, and dispute.

Protect the buyer from repeated outreach, contradictory claims, hidden markups, unclear support, and abandonment between parties.

## Pilot Contract

```text
decision and shared-customer hypothesis
mechanism, parties, products, market, and scope
eligible customer and exclusions
contributions, rights, data, commercial, and operations
exposure, spend, lead, customer, inventory, and support caps
feasibility, first value, recurring value, economics, trust, and service evidence
assignment or comparison, overlap, maturity, and attribution
positive, negative, harmful, and inconclusive rules
stop, rollback, customer protection, exit, and completion proof
```

Avoid broad exclusivity during learning unless a verified constraint and accountable review justify it.
