# Growth Opportunity Sizing Output Contract

## Required Order

Return:

1. mode, decision, owner, as-of date, horizon, scope, entities, market, customer outcome, business value, and external-action boundary;
2. opportunity contract, source ledger, evidence states, contradictions, uncertainty, and unavailable inputs;
3. baseline, eligibility, transition and mechanism map, maturity, formulas, and units;
4. observed baseline or loss, theoretical headroom, reachable change, retained opportunity, and net decision-value ranges;
5. economics, timing, sensitivity, capacity, risk, and failure signals;
6. opportunity relationship graph, gross ranges, attributable adjustments, adjusted portfolio range, and unresolved overlap;
7. `advance`, `validate`, `preserve`, `defer`, `reject`, or `route` decisions with owners, gates, expiry, and review;
8. validation plan, specialist handoffs, pinned Growth Playbook sources, approval-ready external actions, and completion record.

## Opportunity Register

| Opportunity | Customer value and mechanism | Baseline and eligibility | Gross headroom | Reachable retained range | Net decision-value range | Evidence and sensitivity | Owner and review |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Show units and calculations. Use `unavailable` when a required bridge has no defensible bound.

## Factor Ledger

| Factor | Definition and unit | Low / central / high | Evidence state and source | Maturity and freshness | Sensitivity | Contradiction or limitation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Do not mix actual, benchmark, estimate, causal estimate, scenario, forecast, target, plan, or result.

## Overlap Register

| Opportunity pair or group | Shared population, state, value, cost, capacity, or dependency | Relationship | Evidence | Adjustment | Unresolved effect | Decision rule |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Keep gross, adjusted, and unresolved portfolio values separate. Do not use an arbitrary overlap discount.

## Decision Register

| Opportunity | Class | Customer and economic rationale | Dominant uncertainty | Evidence gate | Capacity and dependency | Expiry / scale / cap / stop | Owner |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Use `advance`, `validate`, `preserve`, `defer`, `reject`, or `route`. Do not substitute an unsupported composite score.

## Validation And Handoff Register

| Uncertainty or workstream | Decision changed | Input contract | Method, Skill, or owner | Expected evidence or artifact | Permission boundary |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Do not imply that a handoff, test, implementation, or business result occurred.

## Completion Gate

Confirm that mode, decision, owner, as-of date, horizon, scope, exclusions, product, model, market, locale, entities, participants, segments, customer value, business value, baseline, eligibility, transitions, identity, denominator, window, maturity, source, evidence state, formulas, headroom, reach, adoption, retention, economics, timing, saturation, sensitivity, uncertainty, overlap, cannibalization, interaction, capacity, dependency, risk, privacy, market transfer, classification, evidence gate, expiry, handoff, pinned source, and external action are explicit; targets did not determine assumptions; a benchmark did not become causal uplift; market size did not become company opportunity; attributed outcomes did not become incrementality; gross value did not become contribution or cash; incompatible units were not blended; portfolio values were not double counted; false precision, approvals, implementation, and results were not invented; and no external action occurred.
