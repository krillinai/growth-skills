# Programmatic SEO

Design scalable page systems that repeatedly satisfy specific search intent with reliable data, useful page-level value, a coherent product path, and sustainable maintenance. Treat page count, generation speed, indexed URLs, impressions, and rankings as operating evidence, not customer or business success.

Read [system-contract.md](references/system-contract.md) before approving a page opportunity, data source, template, or URL inventory. Read [quality-and-launch.md](references/quality-and-launch.md) before sampling pages, defining indexability, sequencing a launch, measuring results, or retiring a page family. Use [playbook-sources.md](references/playbook-sources.md) to cite the pinned Growth Playbook basis.

## Select One Mode

| Mode | Use |
| --- | --- |
| `validate` | Decide whether a proposed page family has distinct demand, reliable supply, useful output, product fit, and maintainable economics |
| `design` | Produce the page-family, field, source, template, URL, link, quality, launch, measurement, and ownership contracts |
| `audit` | Evaluate a live or proposed system for usefulness, duplication, empty states, integrity, discoverability, indexation, risk, downstream value, and maintenance |

Name one primary mode. Keep demand evidence, page specification, generated candidate, published page, crawlable page, canonical page, indexed page, qualified visit, activated customer, retained value, and business outcome separate.

## Freeze The System Boundary

Record decision and owner; customer, job, search intent, market, language, locale, device, product and business model; page family and entities; candidate source and known count; demand evidence and date; field sources and licenses; template and variant; URL rules; discovery and canonical rules; page states; product action and downstream value; capacity, cost, refresh, deletion, risk, rollout, and review date.

Start from an approved bounded candidate inventory or a reproducible generation rule. Do not invent demand, keywords, entities, locations, products, comparisons, prices, reviews, availability, attributes, URLs, market size, traffic, rank, conversion, or outcomes.

## Validate The Value Mechanism

Require this chain:

```text
specific intent -> trustworthy structured supply -> useful page transformation
-> indexable logged-out experience -> qualified next action
-> first and retained value -> maintenance and risk control
```

Reject a system that only swaps tokens, paraphrases the same text, reproduces a feed without added utility, creates unsupported comparisons, invents local presence, or publishes pages whose core fields are missing. A template must make the underlying data more useful through selection, computation, comparison, explanation, workflow, availability, or another attributable transformation.

## Specify Page Families And States

For every family define intent and exclusions; primary entity and stable ID; field name, meaning, type, source, freshness, license, null and conflict rules; unique value; common and conditional modules; claim and proof trace; page title, heading and metadata inputs; URL, canonical, locale and pagination rules; internal-link eligibility and anchors; structured-data handoff; CTA and product path; accessibility; and owner.

Define state behavior for valid, sparse, no result, duplicate, conflicting, unavailable, expired, discontinued, merged, removed, unsafe, unreviewed, and restricted pages. Each state needs response, indexability, canonical, sitemap, navigation, link, customer experience, refresh, and retirement rules.

## Gate Quality Before Indexing

Use deterministic validation for schema and completeness, plus human or expert review where usefulness, truth, safety, localization, or claim context requires judgment. Sample across high and low supply, common and rare entities, markets, locales, edge cases, generated combinations, and failure states.

Do not use a universal word count, uniqueness percentage, page count, traffic threshold, click depth, or indexed ratio. Define page-family-specific acceptance from intent satisfaction, reliable fields, distinct value, internal consistency, accessibility, product continuity, and risk. Similar templates can support distinct useful pages; lexical difference alone cannot.

## Launch In Evidence-Bounded Batches

Launch planning begins with a holdout or small representative batch, not every candidate. Freeze inventory and versions; validate raw and rendered output; discovery, status, robots, canonical, locale, sitemap, and internal links; page-state behavior; performance and mobile experience; analytics and downstream event contracts; and rollback.

Expand only when the batch meets explicit quality, discovery, indexation, qualified demand, product-value, cost, support, trust, and search-risk rules at the required maturity. Do not infer incrementality from attribution or organic growth from indexed volume.

## Operate And Retire

Monitor source freshness, schema drift, missingness, duplicates, conflicts, render failures, crawl and index states, query-page fit, qualified arrivals, product progression, retained cohorts, contribution, support, policy or trust signals, update cost, and incident volume. Preserve definitions and comparable page cohorts.

Create ownership for source, template, search, product path, measurement, review, incidents, and removal. Retire or consolidate pages and families when supply disappears, value is no longer distinct, intent changes, product continuity breaks, maintenance exceeds value, risk rises, or evidence rejects the hypothesis. Removal requires exact disposition and validation, not mass redirect to the homepage.

## Deliver In Order

Return:

1. mode, decision, owner, system boundary, candidate inventory, and external-action boundary;
2. intent, demand, supply, product, downstream-value, and risk evidence ledger;
3. page-family and entity model;
4. field-source, license, freshness, null, conflict, and claim contract;
5. template modules, page states, URL, canonical, locale, discovery, sitemap, and internal-link rules;
6. quality gates, deterministic checks, review sample, and acceptance matrix;
7. pilot, holdout, launch, monitoring, rollback, incident, and retirement plan;
8. measurement from candidate through retained value, with attribution and incrementality separated;
9. economics, capacity, ownership, handoffs, and pinned Playbook sources.

Route site hierarchy and migration maps to `search-site-planner`, technical and live-page diagnosis to `seo-audit`, structured data to `structured-data-builder`, landing conversion to `landing-page-audit`, content governance to `content-strategy` when available, causal validation to `experiment-design`, and product or CMS implementation to separately authorized engineering work.

For China work, keep market, language, locale, search provider, product surface, domain, app distribution, mapping or location source, content license, payment, identity, consent, data access, and applicable review separate. Do not infer Baidu behavior, indexing, demand, platform availability, local presence, license, or policy equivalence from China or Simplified Chinese.

This Skill creates and reads local plans and bounded samples only. Do not perform broad crawling, scrape restricted sources, copy third-party content, generate or publish a full inventory, edit CMS or templates, change robots, canonicals, sitemaps, links, redirects, or DNS, request indexing, access private webmaster or analytics systems, deploy, or claim pages are indexed without separate task-level authorization.

## Completion Gate

Confirm that intent, demand, supply, entities, fields, provenance, license, freshness, unique value, templates, states, URLs, canonicals, locale, discovery, links, sitemaps, indexability, quality, sampling, claims, accessibility, product path, downstream value, measurement, economics, capacity, monitoring, rollback, incidents, retirement, owners, and versions are explicit; thinness is judged by utility rather than word count; missing data does not create fictional pages; indexed volume is not treated as growth; provider assumptions remain bounded; and no external action occurred.
