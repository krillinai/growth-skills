# Output Contract

Return Markdown unless the user requests another local format. Keep sources, observed values, reported estimates, inference, scenarios, targets, forecasts, unavailable inputs, and causal claims separate.

## 1. Decision And Market Header

State mode, decision, owner, customer and economic units, job, category, product, geography, period, currency, value basis, source cutoff, model version, review date, non-goals, and external-action boundary.

## 2. Contract And Source Ledger

| ID | Claim or value | Entity and layer | Category, geography, period | Source and lineage | Method and coverage | State | Limitation, overlap, next check |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Include access, license, date, revisions, contradictions, derivative sources, privacy, and unavailable evidence.

## 3. Market Layers

| Layer | Definition | Eligible units | Inclusion and exclusion | Serviceability or reach condition | Value basis | Estimate or range | Uncertainty |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Show bounded universe, total demand, serviceable, reachable, qualified, and attainable layers. Map TAM, SAM, SOM, or internal names explicitly.

## 4. Method Models

For every top-down, bottom-up, supply, transaction, value-pool, or observed-demand view provide equation, source values, conversion bridges, frequency, quantity, price or value, coverage, overlap, assumptions, uncertainty, and result.

## 5. Reconciliation

| Method | Comparable construct | Estimate or range | Independent lineage | Overlap | Main uncertainty | Residual | Decision use |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Explain disagreement and preserve separate views when definitions remain incompatible. Do not average by default.

## 6. Scenarios And Sensitivity

Return current estimate, attainable scenarios, ranges, break-even values, dominant assumptions, sensitivity, triggers, counterevidence, constraints, and conditions under which the decision changes. Keep target, plan, company forecast, and actual separate.

## 7. Governance And Handoffs

List source gaps, refresh and expiry, actualization, privacy, market transfer, model revisions, decision owner, threshold, downside of error, specialist handoffs, and approval-ready next actions.

## 8. Completion Record

Confirm pinned Playbook sources, unit compatibility, nonoverlap, market-layer definitions, evidence states, uncertainty, unavailable inputs, causal boundary, no fabricated precision, and no unauthorized access, purchase, contact, publication, system change, deployment, or result claim.
