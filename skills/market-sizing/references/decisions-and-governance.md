# Decisions And Governance

Market size informs a bounded decision. It does not approve market entry, product scope, positioning, pricing, budgets, hiring, fundraising claims, or forecasts.

## Decision Brief

For each decision state:

- decision, owner, deadline, horizon, and reversibility;
- market layer and exact definition used;
- estimate, range, uncertainty, and dominant assumptions;
- threshold or evidence that changes the decision;
- serviceability, reachability, capacity, cash, customer, trust, and risk constraints;
- downside of over- and underestimation;
- specialist owner and separately authorized next action;
- refresh, actualization, review, and retirement.

Use `evidence acquisition`, `compare`, `validate`, `specialist review`, `hold`, `refresh`, or `retire estimate` as workflow states. These are not operating approvals.

The sizing model preserves each specialist output's entity, method, population, market, period, version, uncertainty, and limitations.

## Causal And Share Boundary

A market estimate rising after company revenue rises, or vice versa, does not establish causality. Preserve price, mix, acquisition, retention, product, competitive, category, currency, accounting, and measurement alternatives.

Calculate share only when company numerator and market denominator use compatible product, customer, geography, period, currency, accounting, and value definitions. Keep observed share, attributed result, estimated share, attainable scenario, target, and forecast separate.

## Privacy And Ethical Sources

Use the minimum data required. Prefer public, licensed, supplied, aggregate, redacted, pseudonymous, and small-cell-suppressed evidence. Record purpose, authority, license, provenance, access, retention, deletion, correction, and audit.

Do not request payment credentials, tax IDs, protected traits, health or financial details, exact locations, private messages, personal contacts, complete purchase histories, device graphs, or customer identities when aggregate evidence is sufficient. Do not identify or rank prospects, reidentify people, bypass controls, copy unlicensed reports, deceive sources, or use leaked data.

## Market Transfer And China

Revalidate market, language, locale, customer, role, job, category, product and version, alternatives, channel, platform, app distribution, frequency, price, payment, tax, invoice, currency, exchange rate, data source, identity, consent, storage, transfer, serviceability, reachability, capacity, and ownership.

For China, require current direct and locally accountable sources for time-sensitive product, platform, payment, app-store, data, advertising, and regulatory conditions. Translation and currency conversion do not validate a local market. Do not provide legal, tax, accounting, or compliance conclusions.

## Source And Model Governance

Version market contract, sources, transformations, filters, overlap rules, assumptions, model, scenarios, overrides, outputs, decisions, and actualization. Preserve historical estimates and explain revisions. Track stale or derivative sources, unresolved overlap, method disagreement, estimate use, decision errors, and whether new evidence changed the decision.

Prepare local models, source requests, comparison briefs, and approval-ready specialist handoffs only. Do not access systems, buy research, contact people, change plans, publish estimates, or claim results without separate authorization.
