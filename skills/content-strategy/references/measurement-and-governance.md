# Measurement And Governance

## Measures By Role

| Role | Immediate evidence | Downstream validation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Category education | qualified reach, comprehension, return, branded or category exploration | qualified demand, correct expectations, retained source cohorts |
| Demand capture | query or intent match, qualified visit, useful completion | product entry, activation, retention, contribution |
| Product proof | demo completion, proof engagement, objection resolution | qualified progression, activation for promised use, trust |
| Customer enablement | task completion, time to resolution, successful setup | first value, repeated value, support quality, retention |
| Sales enablement | asset use, buyer engagement, objection coverage | stage progression, decision quality, win/loss, implementation success |
| Public participation | accepted useful contribution, trusted response, relevant relationships | qualified demand, partnership, reputation, durable community value |

For every metric freeze entity, eligibility, event, denominator, source, identity, window, maturity, segment, market, attribution type, cost scope, guardrails, and owner. A view, like, share, rank, click, lead, or influenced-opportunity label is not downstream value by itself.

## Attribution And Causality

Keep platform credit, first or last touch, multi-touch allocation, self-reported influence, sales usage, cohort association, and causal incrementality separate. Use experiments or the strongest feasible comparison when allocation depends on causal contribution. Never sum overlapping attribution views.

## Content Experiments

Define decision, hypothesis, audience, unit, eligibility, control or baseline, treatment, source and channel, primary role metric, downstream outcome, guardrails, assignment or comparison, exposure, sample and power where applicable, window, maturity, decision rule, stop, rollback, and learning owner. Do not promise uplift.

## Rights And Privacy

Record authorship, license, permitted media, territories, term, attribution, edits, derivatives, model or training permission, customer consent, creator release, testimonial scope, music, footage, image, data, and expiration. Public availability does not grant reuse rights.

Minimize personal data and avoid copying private community content, raw customer messages, identifiable support records, or restricted sources. Use exact quotes only with attributable supplied text and appropriate permission; label paraphrase and translation.

## Market And Channel Governance

Validate local questions, alternatives, terminology, trust, proof, product availability, channel access, format, permissions, content rules, moderation, ownership, and measurement. Translation alone does not establish a local content strategy.

## Refresh And Retirement

Set triggers for product, price, source, evidence, policy, market, competitor, channel, rights, performance, accessibility, or customer-question change. Retired content needs URL or asset disposition, link and distribution cleanup, archive or deletion rules, rights handling, analytics annotation, customer impact review, and validation.
