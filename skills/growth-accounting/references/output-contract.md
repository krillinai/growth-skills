# Output Contract

Return Markdown unless the user requests another local format. Keep absolute movements, rates, adjustments, evidence, interpretations, and external actions distinct.

## 1. Decision Header

State mode, decision, owner, entity and level, active or value state, opening and closing periods, frequency, timezone, observation and maturity windows, product, segment, cohort, market, value basis, currency, as-of, source versions, review date, and external-action boundary.

## 2. Contract And Evidence Ledger

| Input or rule | Entity and definition | Scope and period | Source and version | State | Quality, maturity, limitation | Owner and next check |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Include identity, eligibility, class precedence, history coverage, late and censored states, values, adjustments, lineage, privacy, and unavailable evidence.

## 3. Classification And QA

Show retained, new, resurrected, churned, reactivated when relevant, transfers, migrations, adjustments, unresolved, and excluded populations. Include overlap, missing-history, identity, maturity, and classification checks.

## 4. Movement Bridges

| Movement | Opening value | Additions | Reductions | Adjustments | Closing value | Residual | Evidence state |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Provide separate compatible bridges for entities, recurring value, revenue, profit, or contribution. Show formulas and reconciliation in absolute units.

## 5. Metric Table

| Metric | Numerator | Denominator | Entity and value basis | Window and maturity | Result | Limitation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Do not fabricate missing values, targets, benchmark thresholds, precision, or health scores.

## 6. Comparison And Diagnosis

Show compatible cohort, segment, product, plan, price, market, channel, source, and version views; absolute and rate changes; base and mix effects; concentration; constant- and reported-currency differences; organic and acquired movement; counterevidence; alternatives; and unresolved residuals.

## 7. Decisions And Governance

Return observed findings, bounded interpretations, smallest discriminating evidence, specialist handoffs, owners, refresh and revision triggers, privacy controls, market-transfer limits, approval-ready external actions, and stop or fallback rules.

## 8. Completion Record

List pinned Playbook sources, unavailable evidence, unresolved classes, residuals, next checks, review dates, and completion proof. Confirm that classes were mutually exclusive; immature and missing did not become churn or zero; incomplete history did not become new; incompatible values were not combined; decomposition did not become attribution or causality; and no system or customer state changed.
