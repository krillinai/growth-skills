# Governance And Boundaries

Growth accounting is a versioned analytical service. It does not operate source systems or approve downstream decisions.

## Specialist Ownership

| Need | Route |
| --- | --- |
| Metric constellation, North Star, target, lineage, and governance | `growth-metrics-design` |
| Cohort construction, age matrices, and maturity comparison | `cohort-analysis` |
| Recurring-value, churn, resurrection, and retention mechanisms | `retention` |
| Full stocks, flows, loops, economics, and business constraint model | `growth-model-design` |
| Acquisition touchpoint credit and model reconciliation | `attribution-analysis` |
| Channel strategy, marginal economics, and budget allocation | `acquisition-strategy` |
| Intervention causality and experimental evidence | `experiment-design` |
| Future movement forecast and uncertainty | `growth-forecasting` |
| Customer lifetime value | `ltv-analysis` |
| Pipeline, bookings, invoices, revenue, cash, and finance controls | `revops-audit` |

Preserve entity, period, maturity, market, value basis, version, uncertainty, and owner at every handoff.

## Privacy And Fairness

Use the minimum data required. Prefer aggregate, redacted, pseudonymous, consented, and small-cell-suppressed inputs. Define purpose, authority, access, retention, deletion, correction, audit, and incident ownership.

Do not request payment credentials, health or financial details, protected traits, private messages, exact locations, complete device graphs, employee notes, or raw identities when account-period aggregates are enough. Do not publish person-level churn, value, risk, or resurrection labels or use them to route treatment without a separately governed decision policy.

Identity deletion and consent withdrawal can affect reconciliation. Preserve them as governed data states rather than silently recreating identity.

## Market Transfer And China

Revalidate entity, eligibility, product, contract, plan, price, payment, invoice, tax, refund, currency, channel, platform, identity, attribution, consent, data, storage, transfer, source access, dormancy, grace, accounting basis, and ownership by market.

For China, use current direct sources and locally accountable review for time-sensitive platform, payment, invoice, tax, advertising, data, and regulatory conditions. Translation and currency conversion do not validate a transferred bridge. Do not provide legal, tax, accounting, regulatory, or provider conclusions.

## Operating Cadence

Define source cutoff, production, QA, reconciliation, publication, revision, review, archive, and retirement cadences. Track residuals, unresolved share, late-data revision, identity changes, definition drift, small-cell suppression, stale sources, and decision errors. Preserve every released version and its source snapshot.

## External Actions

Local analysis may prepare contracts, mappings, equations, QA checks, bridges, and handoffs. Accessing systems, exporting or joining raw data, changing identities or lifecycle states, writing dashboards, altering forecasts or targets, changing routing, contacting customers, publishing, deploying, and claiming improvement require separate task-level authorization, access controls, review, and rollback.
