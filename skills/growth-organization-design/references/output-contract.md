# Growth Organization Design Output Contract

## Required Order

Return:

1. primary mode, organization decision, sponsor, customer and business outcome, constraint state, scope, horizon, and action boundary;
2. organization contract and evidence ledger;
3. current outcome, decision-flow, capability, dependency, interface, staffing, and maintenance map;
4. model alternatives and central-versus-local decision record;
5. outcome ownership and decision-rights matrix;
6. target team, roles, staffing assumptions, interfaces, durable ownership, and transition specification;
7. initiative portfolio and operating-forum contracts;
8. disaggregated health ledger, risks, triggers, handoffs, and pinned Playbook sources.

## Organization Design Record

| Area | Current evidence | Failure or need | Alternative | Decision | Owner | Boundary | Review trigger |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Use `unavailable` instead of reconstructing private organization facts. Keep current state, reported signal, inference, recommendation, and authorized decision distinct.

## Central-Versus-Local Record

| Work or decision | Repetition and stability | Shared-quality need | Local context | Coordination cost | Shared owner and contract | Local rights and override | Disposition |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Do not centralize accountable customer, product, market, creative, commercial, or risk judgment merely because shared tooling is useful.

## Staffing And Transition

| Capability or role | Work and outcome | Existing coverage | Gap evidence | Load and horizon | Partners and manager | Maintenance duty | Staffing scenario | Start or stop trigger |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

| Transition item | Current owner | Target owner | Affected work or people | Permission or knowledge transfer | Customer and system risk | Communication | Completion proof | Adjustment or rollback |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Scenarios are not hiring, reporting, compensation, or employment actions. Preserve unfinished-work and maintenance ownership before changing the model.

## Privacy And External Action

Use aggregate, redacted, or pseudonymous evidence under a declared purpose and authority boundary. Exclude credentials, direct identifiers, private communications, compensation, health, protected, immigration, precise-location, customer-identity, and unrelated data.

Local organization analysis does not authorize system access, employee or customer exports, reporting-line or permission changes, employment actions, hiring, compensation, budgets, project allocation, calendar changes, notifications, publication, procurement, or production changes. Record required sponsor, employment, labor, privacy, legal, finance, security, local-market, communication, transition, validation, monitoring, adjustment, and rollback controls as handoffs.
