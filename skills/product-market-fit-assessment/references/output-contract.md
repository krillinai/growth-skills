# Product-Market Fit Assessment Output Contract

## Required Order

Return:

1. primary mode, decision, owner, assessment date, and external-action boundary;
2. bounded fit claim and evidence maturity;
3. fit contract, definition version, and observation period;
4. source, evidence-state, quality, privacy, and contradiction ledger;
5. evidence-stack assessment;
6. Four Fits and model-market assumptions where relevant;
7. strongest support, strongest counterevidence, unavailable evidence, and confidence rationale;
8. one decision and bounded evidence or 30-day validation plan;
9. adjacent-Skill handoffs and pinned Growth Playbook sources.

## Bounded Claim

Use this semantic form without forcing unsupported certainty:

> For `[product and version]`, `[customer or participant, segment, role, and use case]` in `[market and geography]`, with `[payer]` and `[natural frequency]`, evidence during `[period]` `[supports / partially supports / does not yet support]` repeated meaningful value at the `[maturity]` stage. The strongest support is `[evidence]`; the strongest counterevidence or missing condition is `[evidence]`; therefore the next decision is `[decision]`.

Do not issue a timeless company-level badge. Do not convert maturity or confidence into a 0-100 score.

## Evidence Ledger

| Layer | Claim or measure | Entity and population | Window or field date | Source | Evidence state | Support or contradiction | Limitation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Include numerators, denominators, sample frame, cohort age, definition, currency, market, and uncertainty where relevant. Preserve `unavailable`, unknown, immature, contradictory, and negative evidence rather than treating them as zero or omitting them.

## Four Fits Table

| Fit | Current evidence | Constraint | Confidence rationale | Evidence needed next |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Keep each hypothesis distinct. If the model-market equation is used, list each supplied or inferred assumption, range, source, date, and sensitivity. Call the result potential annual revenue under assumptions, not a forecast.

## Decision Plan

| Action or evidence task | Hypothesis | Metric or evidence | Owner | Due or maturity date | Decision rule | Guardrail | Completion proof | Route |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Prefer the smallest plan that can change the declared decision. Do not turn an assessment into positioning, product, pricing, channel, messaging, referral, experiment, or production implementation; route those workflows to the relevant Skill.

## Privacy And Action Boundary

Exclude direct identifiers, credentials, raw private payloads, unnecessary sensitive attributes, and unrelated history. Report decision-required aggregate, coded, or pseudonymous evidence under the declared purpose and minimum-cell policy.

Local assessment does not authorize production access, exports, instrumentation changes, customer contact, surveys, incentives, product changes, pricing, campaigns, spend, experiments, publication, messaging, or deployment. Record required access, consent, privacy review, approvals, rollback, monitoring, and audit controls as unresolved handoffs.
