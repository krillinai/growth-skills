# Acquisition Strategy Output Contract

## Required Order

Return:

1. primary mode, acquisition decision, owner, customer, problem, qualified entry, market, horizon, budget or capacity, and action boundary;
2. acquisition contract, evidence ledger, and versioned source taxonomy;
3. source-to-value path, first constrained transition, and alternative explanations;
4. channel-mechanism and channel-model-fit comparison;
5. identity, source, attribution, cohort, incrementality, and downstream-quality ledger;
6. full and marginal economics with formulas, assumptions, maturity, and uncertainty;
7. portfolio, concentration, dependency, market, and capacity scenarios;
8. staged validation, allocation gates, stop and recovery rules, handoffs, and pinned Playbook sources.

## Source And Quality Ledger

| Source and version | Customer and intent | Eligibility and qualified arrival | Identity and new status | First value | Mature retained value | Contribution | Attribution state | Incrementality state | Evidence and limitation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Do not merge incompatible source taxonomies, identities, product versions, cohort windows, or maturity horizons. Use `unavailable` instead of estimating private results from visible activity.

## Channel Comparison

| Channel hypothesis | Mechanism and customer access | Promise, proof, offer, and path | Model fit | Evidence time | Control and capacity | Saturation | Dependency and risk | Current evidence | Disposition and gate |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Use `test`, `keep manual`, `scale band`, `cap`, `redesign`, `reallocate`, or `stop` only with an explicit owner, horizon, decision rule, and stop condition. A recommendation is not a live channel action.

## Economics And Portfolio

| Channel or band | Eligible remaining demand | Incremental customers | Retained contribution | Full incremental cost | Incremental CAC | Payback and cash | Capacity | Concentration and risk | Evidence maturity | Low, base, high scenario | Gate |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Show formulas and cost inclusions. Prevent overlap and double counting. Keep attributed, reconciled, predicted, and incremental values separate. Do not allocate from historical average CAC, platform ROAS, or a composite score.

## Privacy And External Action

Use only decision-required aggregate, redacted, or pseudonymous evidence under a declared purpose and authority boundary. Exclude credentials, direct identifiers, payment credentials, tax IDs, private communications, exact locations, unnecessary protected attributes, complete device graphs, and unrelated histories.

Local strategy does not authorize account access, exports, enrichment, audience uploads, outreach, partner contact, bids, budgets, targeting, offers, prices, pages, campaigns, permissions, tracking, contracts, publishing, market launch, tests, procurement, or production changes. Record required access, privacy, legal, finance, partner, budget, experiment, owner, approval, validation, rollback, monitoring, incident, and audit controls as handoffs.
