# Growth Investment Case Output Contract

## Required Order

Return:

1. mode, case ID, version, decision, owner, scope, horizon, baseline, options, strategy version, evidence cutoff, review date, expiry, and external-action boundary;
2. customer-value and strategic-alignment thesis;
3. source, evidence, contradiction, uncertainty, and missing-input ledger;
4. current-state, do-nothing, preferred, and credible alternative comparison;
5. incremental benefit, cost, contribution, cash, capacity, opportunity-cost, and reconciliation views;
6. timing, maturity, scenario, sensitivity, break-even, and valuation views that supplied evidence supports;
7. risk, dependency, reversibility, customer protection, market-transfer, and residual-obligation register;
8. gate states, first decision-limiting gate, and `approve`, `bounded pilot`, `preserve option`, `defer`, `reject`, or `unresolved` recommendation with conditions;
9. pilot or next-proof plan, governance, approvers, refresh triggers, and decision record;
10. specialist handoffs, relevant pinned Growth Playbook sources, approval-ready external actions, and completion record.

## Evidence Ledger

| ID | Claim or input | Entity, segment, market | Period, version, unit | Value or range | Source and lineage | State | Limitation, contradiction, next check |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Preserve actual, estimate, forecast, scenario, target, benchmark, attribution, causal estimate, approval, zero, and unavailable states.

## Option Register

| Option | Baseline change | Customer and strategic value | Evidence | Benefit and cost basis | Cash and capacity | Risk and reversibility | Opportunity cost | Verdict and trigger |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Include current state and do nothing. Do not create weak alternatives or use incompatible bases to favor one option.

## Incremental Value And Resource Register

| ID | Benefit, cost, or resource | Beneficiary or owner | Baseline | Increment | Timing and maturity | Evidence and causal status | Overlap key | Cash, capacity, and limitation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Reconcile customer, lifecycle, revenue, contribution, cash, capacity, cost reduction, avoidance, risk, and option views without double counting.

## Scenario And Sensitivity Register

| Scenario or input | Baseline or range | Source | Changed assumptions | Outcome and unit | Decision boundary | Trigger | Response |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Do not invent probabilities. Show formulas and substituted values only where input contracts are complete.

## Risk And Gate Register

| Risk or gate | State | Exposure and evidence | Prevention or prerequisite | Mitigation and owner | Threshold or decision rule | Stop, rollback, residual risk |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Keep noncompensable harms and incompatible risks separate. Do not average gates into a score.

## Pilot Or Proof Plan

| Evidence task | Decision uncertainty | Scope and exposure | Baseline or method | Metric and maturity | Owner and date | Positive / negative / harmful / inconclusive rule | Cap / stop / rollback / expiry |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Do not call a full rollout or irreversible commitment a pilot.

## Decision Record

| Recommendation | Rationale | Conditions | Required approvers | Expiry and refresh trigger | Superseded case | External actions not yet authorized |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Recommendation is not approval, authorization, funding, execution, or result.

## Completion Gate

Confirm that case ID, version, mode, decision, owner, scope, horizon, strategy link, baseline, do nothing, credible alternatives, resources, outcomes, customers, value, mechanism, sources, evidence states, contradictions, market layers, attribution, incrementality, benefits, costs, contribution, cash, capacity, maturity, opportunity cost, overlap, scenarios, sensitivity, valuation limits, risks, reversibility, dependencies, gates, recommendation, conditions, pilot, approvers, expiry, refresh, market transfer, privacy, handoffs, pinned sources, and external actions are explicit; sunk cost did not justify future spend; TAM did not become company revenue; attribution did not become causal benefit; gross and net value were not added; lifecycle effects were not double counted; targets did not become evidence; unknowns did not become zero; scenarios did not become forecasts; unsupported precision, probability, approval, authorization, implementation, and results were not invented; and no external action occurred.
