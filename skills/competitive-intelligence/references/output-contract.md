# Output Contract

Use this order. Keep verified evidence, reported signals, bounded observations, provider estimates, customer reports, inference, hypotheses, forecasts, unavailable inputs, approvals, and causal claims visibly distinct.

## 1. Decision Header

State mode, decision, owner, customer and buying unit, job or situation, product, market and locale, horizon, evidence cutoff, and external-action boundary.

## 2. Executive Finding

State what the evidence can and cannot support. Name the primary alternative-set, source, freshness, version, availability, price, customer-evidence, comparability, or causal constraint. Do not return an unexplained score, universal winner, threat, or roadmap prediction.

## 3. Intelligence Contract And Source Inventory

Include companies, products, plans, versions, markets, source records, dates, exact support, evidence states, access conditions, conflicts, limitations, coverage, and privacy boundary.

## 4. Alternative Landscape

Show direct, adjacent, suite, point, open-source, internal, service, manual, status-quo, delay, and no-action alternatives as applicable. Include customer job coverage, inclusion, exclusion, and completeness boundary.

## 5. Capability And Commercial Matrix

Separate claimed, documented, available, eligible, enabled, observed, customer-reported, and outcome-supported states. Show pricing units, minimums, terms, usage, fees, implementation, currency, taxes, effective dates, and unavailable terms without forced normalization.

## 6. Decision Comparison

Compare decision-changing criteria, customer consequence, evidence, proof, limitations, conflicts, best-fit and poor-fit conditions, and sensitivity. Keep unavailable distinct from absent or zero.

## 7. Signals, Changes, And Hypotheses

Show first observed, announced, effective, verified, corrected, reversed, or expired state; materiality; alternative explanations; disconfirming evidence; owner; review date; expiry; and retraction.

## 8. Governance And Handoffs

Return the smallest next evidence, decision trigger, owner, review cadence, stop rule, completion proof, specialist handoff, and pinned Playbook source. Keep external actions approval-ready and unexecuted.

## Mode-Specific Requirements

| Mode | Required addition |
| --- | --- |
| `landscape` | discovery universe, alternative families, inclusion and exclusion, source coverage, and completeness boundary |
| `compare` | frozen customer decision, criterion matrix, commercial scenarios, tradeoffs, missingness, and sensitivity |
| `monitor` | versioned source and change log, confirmation, materiality, expiry, correction, retraction, and decision trigger |

## Final Checks

- Company, product, plan, market, platform, and version are not conflated.
- Vendor claims, observations, customer reports, estimates, inference, and causal conclusions remain separate.
- Unknown is not absent, zero, failure, or a lost comparison.
- Prices are normalized only under compatible declared scenarios.
- No private metric, roadmap, market size, share, score, quote, citation, provider condition, legal conclusion, or causal effect is invented.
- No restricted access, impersonation, scrape, probe, contact, purchase, publication, production change, deployment, or result claim appears.
