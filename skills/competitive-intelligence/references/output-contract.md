# Output Contract

Return Markdown unless the user requests another local format. Preserve evidence states verbatim.

## 1. Scope And Decision

State mode, decision, owner, product and version, customer and buying unit, job and trigger, market, language, locale, segment, alternative boundary, criteria, cutoff, horizon, review date, exclusions, and external-action boundary.

## 2. Source And Evidence Ledger

| ID | Claim or observation | Entity and version | Market and scope | Source and lineage | Dates | State | Freshness | Contradiction and limitation | Next check |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Show inaccessible, stale, disputed, and unavailable evidence. Keep reported signals separate from evidence states.

## 3. Alternative Landscape

| Alternative | Type | Customer job and situation | Included scope | Why considered | Evidence | Unknown or exclusion boundary |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Include direct, adjacent, general tool, internal build, manual, service, partner, and inaction alternatives where relevant. Do not imply completeness.

## 4. Decision Comparison

| Criterion | Customer consequence | Alternative and state | Plan, market, and version | Evidence | Tradeoff | Blocker or unknown |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Separate capability, availability, quality, integration, offer, price, distribution, proof, trust, switching, and outcome evidence. If prices are compared, include original offers and each normalized scenario. If scoring is used, provide weights, evidence coverage, missingness, blocker rules, and sensitivity outside the total.

## 5. Change Log

| Change | Prior state | Current state | Product, offer, and market | Dates | Evidence | Confidence | Consequence hypothesis | Counterinterpretation | Owner, expiry, next check |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Keep rumors and scenarios in a separate verification queue. Never present them as observed change.

## 6. Implications And Decisions

For each implication provide verified observation, inferred mechanism, counterinterpretations, affected decision, consequence and reversibility, evidence that would change the view, workflow state, owner, review date, and specialist handoff. Do not use intelligence to claim causality or approve execution.

## 7. Governance And Next Evidence

List privacy and access controls, ethical restrictions, source gaps, refresh and expiry rules, local-market validation, archive policy, decision log, and the smallest next evidence that can resolve a consequential gap.

## 8. Completion Record

Confirm pinned Playbook sources, evidence-state compliance, contradictions retained, source and date lineage, market and version boundaries, unavailable inputs, causal limits, specialist routes, approval-ready external actions, and that no unauthorized access, contact, publication, system change, spend, deployment, or result claim occurred.
