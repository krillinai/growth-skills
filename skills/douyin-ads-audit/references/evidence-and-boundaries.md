# Douyin Ads Evidence And Boundaries

## Evidence States

Use `verified`, `reported signal`, `inferred`, `unavailable`, or `not applicable` for each material claim. Verification means a supplied attributable source supports the statement within compatible scope and date. It does not establish causality, permission, representativeness, or customer value beyond that scope.

Missing account or private business data is not poor performance. Request the smallest privacy-safe evidence that changes the decision.

## Claim Controls

- International TikTok evidence does not establish Douyin, Ocean Engine, Qianchuan, or DOU+ behavior.
- An Ocean Engine multi-placement row does not establish Douyin-only delivery.
- Platform attribution does not establish incrementality, contribution, payback, retention, or customer quality.
- Attributed order or GMV does not establish paid order, net revenue, contribution, or profit.
- Organic content performance does not establish paid effect.
- A Douyin account, creator, content, Xingtu, or authorization label does not prove ownership, endorsement, claim authorship, rights, or effectiveness.
- A hook, completion, conversion, cost, ROI, or GMV movement does not establish its cause.
- A screenshot verifies only its visible surface, scope, and capture time.
- External benchmarks require compatible source surface, placement, market, objective, definition, attribution, order maturity, and economics.

Preserve counterevidence and cite local files, row ranges, captures, and dates.

## Unsupported Precision

Never invent universal video duration, opening-seconds rate, completion rate, creative, account, or creator count, frequency, fatigue, conversion, cost, ROI, GMV, budget, bid, audience size, scaling rule, stopping rule, attribution window, order window, or incremental effect.

When economics are incomplete, return the blocked decision, scenario formula, missing inputs, and evidence plan instead of a temporary platform threshold.

## Read-Only And Security Boundary

This Skill may read supplied local files, validate a local bundle, calculate descriptive measures, compare dated delivery, creative, creator-rights, destination, measurement, commerce, and business evidence, and write local findings or experiment plans.

It must not access advertising, shop, creator, Xingtu, CRM, analytics, or payment systems; ask for or use credentials or authorization codes; call a live API; create, edit, publish, boost, pause, or delete entities; change identity, placements, targeting, bids, budgets, optimization, audiences, events, products, shops, livestreams, or destinations; upload customer, lead, audience, or event data; contact creators or leads; or claim an action occurred.

Do not process direct identifiers, raw leads, device or click identifiers linked to people, IP addresses, audience lists, raw event payloads, customer-level orders, payment records, credentials, tokens, secrets, or authorization codes. Stop using the affected artifact without reproducing sensitive values and request a redacted or aggregated replacement.

An execution or international TikTok request uses `out-of-scope`. Preserve supported work and list the separate platform capability, authorization, advertising and platform review, creator rights, privacy, approval, monitoring, rollback, and audit-log controls required.
