# Douyin Ads Audit Methods

Analyze compatible supplied evidence only. Use the organization's own history and declared economics rather than imported TikTok or universal platform thresholds.

## Dependency Order

```text
decision, source surface, and inventory scope
-> conversion and input integrity
-> structure and paid delivery
-> creative, creator, product, and livestream context
-> destination continuity
-> commerce and business reconciliation
-> bounded findings and decision plan
```

Continue with supported sections when evidence is partial, but name blocked decisions.

## Platform Surfaces And Delivery

Use stable IDs to inventory source surface, campaign, ad group, ad, creative, account, placement, optimization goal, destination, schedule, status, and result definition. Keep Ocean Engine, Qianchuan, and DOU+ distributions separate unless compatible definitions and joins support a combined business view. Keep Xingtu as cooperation or rights evidence, not an advertising-delivery row.

Possible descriptive measures include spend share, CPM, click-through rate, cost per click, declared video-view rates, platform conversion rate, platform cost per result, attributed order cost, and attributed GMV per spend. Show denominator, date, source surface, placement, format, currency, result type, and attribution setting. A zero denominator yields `unavailable`.

Do not call multi-placement delivery Douyin-only without a compatible placement breakdown. Concentration, platform recommendations, system labels, and optimization status are signals, not business verdicts.

## Creative Concepts And Variants

Map persona, situation, problem, promise, proof, offer, price, CTA, Douyin account, creator, hook, narrative, music, caption, language, format, duration, product, livestream context, destination, and rights. Treat a changed customer belief or value hypothesis as a possible new concept; treat alternate openings, edits, crops, lengths, captions, subtitles, music, and aspect ratios as variants when the hypothesis is unchanged.

Attention and completion measures are platform activity signals under exported definitions, not customer value. Do not impose international TikTok heuristics or universal opening-seconds, completion, duration, creator, or creative-volume rules.

### Fatigue Signals

Require a comparable time series and delivery context. Examine sustained movement in attention, qualified outcomes, cost, repeated reach, concept diversity, account or creator mix, placement, audience, destination, product, price, livestream schedule, seasonality, auction, and measurement. Use `fatigue signal` unless evidence distinguishes creative decay from other causes.

## Accounts, Creators, And Xingtu

Separate visible account or content identity, supplied cooperation status, authorization scope and expiry, organic performance, paid delivery, creator cost, commerce result, and incrementality. A public video, creator appearance, Xingtu record, or existing engagement does not establish ownership, endorsement, claim authorship, music rights, perpetual usage, or causal value.

Never request, store, redeem, renew, or expose an authorization code or token. Do not contact a creator.

## Commerce And Livestreams

Separate attributed order, paid order, cancellation, refund, GMV, gross revenue, platform fee, commission, product cost, fulfillment, variable cost, contribution, and retained customer value. Apply compatible order and maturation windows. Livestream outcomes also require schedule, host, product, inventory, price, offer, traffic source, capacity, and operational context.

Do not treat GMV as revenue, attributed GMV as incremental GMV, or ROI labels as profit. Do not infer a product, creator, or livestream mechanism from aggregate Qianchuan rows alone.

## Destination Continuity

Compare the dated ad with the landing, app, native form, shop, product, or livestream audience state, promise, price, promotion, proof, CTA, language, device path, inventory, schedule, and next value. Mark speed, form, checkout, deep-link, shop, livestream, consent, and tracking problems inferred until measured. Never edit or operate a destination.

## Measurement And Reconciliation

Build a ledger for conversion name, source surface, source, optimization status, counting, value, attribution setting, timezone, currency, identity, deduplication, consent, new-customer logic, lead qualification, order state, refunds, maturation, and business definition.

Keep these layers separate:

1. paid delivery reported by Ocean Engine, Qianchuan, or DOU+;
2. platform-attributed web, app, lead, shop, product, order, or livestream outcomes;
3. organic Douyin or creator evidence;
4. Xingtu cooperation and rights evidence;
5. business-system qualified customers, revenue, refunds, contribution, and retained value;
6. incremental outcomes supported by a credible counterfactual.

Do not force layers to match. Reconcile definitions, dates, source surfaces, placements, windows, modeled or view credit, imports, identity, deduplication, consent, currency, leads, cancellations, refunds, late orders, creator costs, and unavailable records. Attribution and organic lift are not incrementality.

## Reported Change Diagnosis

Translate a reported change into metric, definition, entity, source surface, placement, market, format, product or livestream scope, comparison window, attribution, magnitude, and source. Mark it `reported signal` until verified. Check:

1. exports, conversion definitions, attribution, tracking, consent, and data changes;
2. maturity, dates, seasonality, policy, product, price, inventory, and livestream events;
3. spend, auction, placement, audience, optimization, and delivery mix;
4. concepts, variants, accounts, creators, rights, products, and destinations;
5. qualified customer, refunds, contribution, capacity, and retained value;
6. causal evidence.

Return competing explanations and the smallest discriminating evidence. Do not jump from correlation to a creative, creator, product, audience, bid, budget, or campaign action.

## Findings

For each finding include observation, decision impact, source and date, evidence state, bounded action, completion proof, owner, and relative effort. Sequence the 30-day plan by evidence dependency. Use declared economics or prospectively agreed experiment rules; never fabricate thresholds.
