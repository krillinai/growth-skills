# Framework Reference

## Retention Evidence for PMF

Choose a natural value interval and inspect mature cohorts by segment, source, use case, and product state. A flattening curve identifies a retained group, not a universal PMF certificate. Explain who retains, why, and whether the behavior supports viable economics.

## Setup-Aha-Habit Onboarding

```text
Setup -> Aha -> Habit -> Retention
```

- **Setup:** obtain the minimum data, inventory, connection, or configuration needed for value.
- **Aha:** deliver the core value rather than registration completion.
- **Habit:** create a credible path to repeated value at the natural cadence.

Measure step conversion and downstream cohort retention. Removing friction is harmful when the removed step prepares value.

## Product-Specific Growth Model

Map acquisition, activation, retention, monetization, and loops as causal transitions. For each edge record rate, quality, cycle time, cost, saturation, and owner. Select the current limiting transition rather than collecting frameworks or tactics.

## Supply as Product

Define whether supply must be comprehensive, exclusive, or curated. Measure relevant availability, quality, match or fill rate, time to fulfillment, participant economics, concentration, multi-homing, and repeat behavior within the actual geographic or categorical unit.

## Repeatable Marketplace Unit

Before expansion, verify retained and economically healthy value in the current unit. Test shared demand, shared supply, operating capabilities, time to liquidity, subsidy dependency, and whether expansion strengthens or fragments the core.

## SEO Product System

```text
structured supply or useful content
-> indexable page for real intent
-> valuable logged-out experience
-> qualified conversion
-> activation and retention
```

Evaluate page uniqueness, intent match, freshness, indexation, conversion quality, retained cohorts, and platform risk. Traffic alone is not success.

## Actual Entry-Surface Conversion

Rank pages by qualified entries, then inspect comprehension, next action, activation, retention, and trust. Test the surface users actually enter, not only the homepage. A signup wall can raise registrations while lowering understanding and retained value.

## Kindle-to-Fire Transition

Use manual “kindle” work to find a retained segment and learn the mechanism. Move to scalable “fire” channels only when the segment, value, channel fit, operational repeatability, and marginal economics are supported by evidence.

## Constraint-Aligned Ownership

Define the outcome and constraint before the team shape. Match decision rights, data access, execution capability, dependencies, and maintenance burden. Choose temporary generalist ownership or durable specialization based on the work, not maturity theater.

## AI Trust System

For products representing a person's expertise, require source traceability, attribution, consent, approval, redaction, correction, feedback, and withdrawal. Measure faithful usefulness and user control alongside engagement and distribution.
