## Attribution Rules

- Treat advisory examples as partial observations, not complete internal accounts.
- Treat short clips as context-limited and prefer full talks when possible.
- Do not attribute company outcomes to one person or framework.
- Do not imply endorsement, private access, or current company facts.
- Preserve first-party product evidence when it conflicts with the framework.
