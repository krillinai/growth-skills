# Campaign Contract

## Minimum Context

Capture these fields independently:

- decision, owner, campaign type, campaign dates, decision date, and review date;
- objective, metric definition, baseline, target, qualified unit, segment, and maturation window;
- customer, job, intent or trigger, buying or use situation, role, lifecycle state, market, language, locale, and exclusions;
- product, value, alternatives, positioning, promise, proof, offer, price, incentive, CTA, and claim sources;
- channel inventory, channel role, account ownership, permissions, policy and platform dependencies, and current evidence;
- owned entry surface, continuity, next action, first value, retained value, and sales or service handoff;
- lifecycle eligibility, consent, suppression, fatigue, frequency, collision, quiet-hour, and completion rules;
- asset formats, variants, locales, accessibility, brand, legal, platform, and approval requirements;
- budget, currency, cost scope, capacity, allocation assumptions, and downside limits;
- source taxonomy, UTM or campaign IDs, identity, attribution, incrementality, experiment, cohort, and reconciliation rules;
- owners, dependencies, due dates, QA, launch, monitoring, stop, rollback, incident, reporting, and postmortem plan;
- source IDs, capture dates, definitions, populations, and limitations.

## Evidence And Claims

For every audience, value, performance, customer, comparative, pricing, availability, legal, security, environmental, or outcome claim record exact wording, source, owner, market, date, scope, evidence state, limitation, approval, and expiration or review date.

Do not convert product aspirations, internal estimates, customer anecdotes, model output, or public competitor claims into verified campaign claims. Keep proof appropriate to the channel and landing experience.

## Permissions And Privacy

Separate audience eligibility, lawful or policy basis, channel permission, account access, consent, suppression, upload rights, retargeting permission, data joins, identity resolution, and publication approval. Existing customer status does not automatically authorize every campaign or channel.

Use aggregate or pseudonymous planning data whenever direct identity is unnecessary. Exclude raw emails, phone numbers, names, private URLs, credentials, customer lists, payment data, sensitive attributes, and unrelated histories from deliverables. Do not claim an audience was uploaded, suppressed, contacted, or deleted.

## Output Boundary

If a required field is unavailable, return the campaign contract, assumptions, scenarios, evidence requests, and blocked execution items. Do not fabricate access, approval, benchmarks, budget performance, or launch readiness.
