# Market Entry Contract

## Intake

Capture independently:

- decision, owner, approver, horizon, investment and capacity boundary, review cadence, and allowed action;
- country, region, city, language, locale, timezone, calendar, currency, and market unit;
- product, edition, module, service, package, price, offer, product surface, device, and distribution state;
- customer, user, buyer, payer, administrator, beneficiary, implementation, support, and contracting units;
- problem, trigger, job, urgency, frequency, current alternative, switching cost, prerequisites, first value, repeated value, and exclusions;
- category, terminology, local alternatives, position, promise, proof, trust, claims, objections, and prohibited wording;
- website, app store, marketplace, search, social, paid, owned, earned, sales, product-led, event, affiliate, and partner paths;
- channel and platform access, account, permission, format, policy, data, dependency, saturation, and recovery;
- price display, currency, payment, settlement, refund, tax and invoice review, contract, discount, contribution, payback, and cash timing;
- availability, localization, accessibility, identity, integrations, performance, content, safety, service, support, moderation, fulfillment, and logistics;
- legal and contracting entity, hosting, data purpose, consent, access, storage, transfer, retention, deletion, security, audit, and incident assumptions;
- research, analytics, pilot, attribution, experiment, customer outcome, operational, financial, and causal evidence;
- source title or artifact, direct URL or locator, publisher or owner, exposed and retrieval date, exact supported claim, evidence state, limitation, and counterevidence.

Version the contract when product, customer, market, route, offer, price, payment, data, support, channel, partner, or review conditions change materially.

## Evidence Boundary

Use `verified`, `inferred`, `unavailable`, or `not applicable`. Use `reported signal` only for an attributable stakeholder statement without inspectable support and leave evidence state blank.

Public evidence can verify visible product availability, local pages, app listings, published prices, current terms, documented payment methods, public channel behavior, public proof, and official text at a dated capture. It cannot verify private demand, customer motives, account access, permission, conversion, retention, economics, capacity, partner commitment, internal approval, or causal impact.

For time-sensitive claims, prefer current direct official sources. Record exact text or bounded claim, publisher, product, market, exposed date, retrieval date, access state, and limitation. Search snippets, blogs, vendor summaries, translation, prior memory, and a status code alone are insufficient.

## Market Unit Test

Split a proposed market when any of these changes the decision:

- problem, frequency, urgency, alternative, buyer, payer, or implementation unit;
- category, terminology, trust, claim, or proof;
- product access, app distribution, device, integration, identity, payment, or support;
- channel, platform, partner, permission, saturation, or acquisition economics;
- city, region, language, locale, currency, contract, tax or invoice review, data, or operations;
- first value, recurring value, retention, contribution, capacity, or owner.

Do not merge populations merely because they share a language, country, platform, or corporate segment label.
