# Customer Expansion Strategy Output Contract

## Required Order

Return:

1. primary mode, decision, owner, customer entity, original scope, expansion type and scope, market, horizon, and action boundary;
2. expansion contract and evidence ledger;
3. original-scope retained-value, reliability, commercial, and capacity gate;
4. adjacent need, roles, readiness, authority, prerequisites, and exclusions;
5. expansion state chain and new-scope, original-scope, and total-customer outcomes;
6. cohort, signal, commercial, cost, contribution, cannibalization, and causal ledger;
7. product-success-commercial handoff or portfolio scenarios;
8. validation, suppression, guardrail, stop, handoff, and pinned Playbook source plan.

## Expansion Ledger

| Customer or cohort | Original retained value | Expansion type and entity | Adjacent need | Readiness and authority | Exposed path | First and repeated new value | Original-scope health | Commercial and contribution | Evidence and decision |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Use `unavailable` instead of reconstructing private account behavior. Do not include direct identifiers in outputs. Keep product adoption, commercial attachment, billing, prediction, attribution, and causal effect separate.

## State Reconciliation

| Type | Eligible | Signaled and exposed | Accepted and provisioned | Activated | First value | Repeated and retained value | Renewed | Contracted and billed | Contraction or churn |

Use one entity, cohort, window, maturity, product and contract version per row. Explain non-reconciling residuals rather than forcing totals.

Recommendations are local specifications, not CRM routing, customer outreach, product changes, pricing, contracts, budget movement, or launch authorization.

## Privacy And External Action

Use decision-required aggregate, redacted, or pseudonymous evidence. Exclude credentials, direct identifiers, compensation, payment credentials, tax IDs, private communications, exact locations, protected attributes, unnecessary organization graphs, executive relationships, and unrelated histories.

Local strategy does not authorize system access, exports, enrichment, scoring, routing, outreach, seat or entitlement changes, usage limits, packages, prices, discounts, contracts, permissions, lifecycle states, ownership, forecasts, dashboards, budgets, offers, messages, experiments, publishing, procurement, or production changes. Record required owner, privacy, security, legal, finance, commercial, budget, experiment, approval, validation, rollback, monitoring, incident, customer-remediation, and audit controls as handoffs.
