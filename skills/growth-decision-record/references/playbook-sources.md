## Source Rules

- Cite the exact pinned page and section used.
- Keep Playbook concepts separate from organization-specific facts and direct evidence.
- Preserve source date, version, market, customer, product, method, maturity, and transfer limits.
- Do not use a case, analogy, framework, or expert statement as causal proof, approval, authorization, or benchmark fit.
- Record current direct sources when a decision depends on changing product, platform, market, legal, regulatory, price, provider, or operating conditions.
- If a newer Playbook version is used, record its commit and do not silently mix it with this snapshot.
