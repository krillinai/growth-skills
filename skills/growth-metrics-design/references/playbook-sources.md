# Growth Playbook Sources

## Pinned Handbook Basis

Use these Growth Playbook pages at commit [`b2ae914`](https://github.com/krillinai/growth-playbook/commit/b2ae914). The pin keeps definitions and reasoning reviewable as the Playbook evolves.

- [Metrics](https://github.com/krillinai/growth-playbook/blob/b2ae914/handbook/metrics/README.md): metric roles, contracts, trees, cohorts, quality, North Star candidates, guardrails, business health, and governance.
- [Growth Model](https://github.com/krillinai/growth-playbook/blob/b2ae914/handbook/growth-model/README.md): connection among customer value, lifecycle, funnels, loops, monetization, constraints, and reinvestment.
- [Funnel & Constraint](https://github.com/krillinai/growth-playbook/blob/b2ae914/handbook/funnel-and-constraint/README.md): value states, denominators, transitions, absolute contribution, and constraint diagnosis.
- [Experimentation](https://github.com/krillinai/growth-playbook/blob/b2ae914/handbook/experimentation/README.md): causal evidence, assignment, maturity, uncertainty, interference, guardrails, and decision rules.
- [Growth Infrastructure](https://github.com/krillinai/growth-playbook/blob/b2ae914/handbook/growth-infrastructure/README.md): instrumentation, identity, data quality, lineage, experimentation platforms, privacy, and operating controls.
- [Retention](https://github.com/krillinai/growth-playbook/blob/b2ae914/handbook/retention/README.md): recurring value, natural intervals, mature cohorts, churn, resurrection, and retained quality.

These chapters provide transferable definitions and questions. They do not verify a private metric, source, baseline, target, benchmark, causal relationship, or result.
