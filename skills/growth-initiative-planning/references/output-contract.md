# Growth Initiative Planning Output Contract

## Required Order

Return:

1. mode, initiative ID and version, plan state, source decision and version, owners, approvers, authority boundary, horizon, market, evidence cutoff, review, expiry, and external-action boundary;
2. evidence, assumptions, contradictions, unavailable inputs, baseline, target, opportunity, forecast, capacity, and approval ledger;
3. bounded customer, product, market, outcome, mechanism, metric, guardrail, scope, non-goal, exclusion, and reconciliation contract;
4. workstream map and specialist handoffs;
5. dependency graph, critical and near-critical paths, unresolved durations, buffers, alternatives, and failure paths;
6. capacity, budget boundary, operating reserve, maintenance, contention, opportunity cost, and feasibility view;
7. evidence and operating gates, milestones, commitments, schedule, completion proof, and decision rules;
8. risks, change control, replan triggers, market transfer, privacy, residual obligations, approval-ready external actions, pinned sources, and completion record.

## Initiative Contract

| Initiative / source decision | Customer, product, market, and model | Baseline / desired outcome / maturity | Scope / non-goals / exclusions | Owner / approver / authority | Version / horizon / cutoff / review / expiry | State and missing readiness input |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Keep actual, target, opportunity, forecast, plan, approval, action, completion, and result distinct.

## Outcome And Evidence Ledger

| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Use `verified`, `inferred`, `unavailable`, or `not applicable`. Do not call activities or outputs impact.

## Commitment And Change Register

| Commitment or change | Owner | Artifact or outcome | Dependency | Due or maturity date | Completion proof or prior state | Current state / decision / expiry | Permission and escalation boundary |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Preserve missed and superseded commitments. Do not use traffic-light status or percent complete as proof.

## Completion Gate

Confirm that mode, plan state, initiative, source decision, version, owner, approver, authority, customer, participant, product, model, market, locale, baseline, target, opportunity, forecast, outcome, mechanism, metrics, evidence, maturity, guardrails, scope, non-goals, exclusions, workstreams, handoffs, dependencies, critical path, capacity, budget, maintenance, opportunity cost, gates, milestones, commitments, dates, proof, risks, assumptions, contradictions, stop, rollback, expiry, change control, replan triggers, review, privacy, market transfer, residual obligations, pinned sources, and external actions are explicit; incompatible units were not blended; missing inputs were not invented; targets were not treated as evidence; activities, dates, status, and ticket closure did not become impact; outcomes were not double counted; capacity was not double-booked; customer protections were not removed to preserve schedule; experiments were not confused with rollouts; history was not rewritten; approvals, execution, completion, and results were not invented; and no external action occurred.
