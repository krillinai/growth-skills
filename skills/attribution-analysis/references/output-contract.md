# Output Contract

Use this order. Keep verified evidence, reported signals, inference, unavailable inputs, scenarios, approvals, and causal claims visibly distinct.

## 1. Decision Header

State mode, operational decision, owner, outcome, entity, product, market and locale, horizon, attribution rule and version, evidence cutoff, and external-action boundary.

## 2. Executive Finding

State what the current attribution view can and cannot support. Name the primary definition, identity, overlap, reconciliation, maturity, model-sensitivity, or causal constraint. Do not return an unexplained score or a winning channel.

## 3. Attribution Contract And Evidence Ledger

Include entities, outcome, source taxonomy, eligible touchpoints, identity, deduplication, direct and unknown rules, windows, precedence, versions, evidence states, contradictions, limitations, and privacy boundary.

## 4. Identity And Coverage Map

Show device-to-user-to-account-to-payer hierarchy as applicable, deterministic and uncertain joins, matched, unmatched, duplicate, ambiguous, consent-denied, deleted, and unknown states, plus coverage rates with declared denominators.

## 5. Journey And Model Analysis

Show eligible journeys, new and returning states, touchpoint roles, current model rule, and candidate model sensitivity. Tie every model to an operational decision and show how assumptions change credit.

## 6. Reconciliation Waterfall

Reconcile platform or partner claims through product, CRM, billing, finance, refunds, contribution, and mature customer value. Preserve overlap, exclusions, reversals, disputed, late, unattributed, and unknown outcomes.

## 7. Quality, Economics, And Causal Boundary

Report coverage, uniqueness, completeness, validity, consistency, timeliness, stability, downstream cohort quality, cost scope, economic state, model quality, counterfactual gaps, and what must route to Experiment Design or Acquisition Strategy.

## 8. Remediation And Governance

Prioritize dependency-ordered fixes with owner, evidence, completion proof, risk, expiry, and revalidation. Include tracking, metrics, RevOps, finance, causal, market, privacy, and system handoffs. Keep changes approval-ready and unexecuted.

## Mode-Specific Requirements

| Mode | Required addition |
| --- | --- |
| `audit` | finding ledger with observed symptom, evidence, alternatives, decision consequence, and bounded response |
| `design` | implementation-ready attribution rule, identity, source, journey, quality, validation, governance, and migration contract |
| `reconcile` | entity map, source-truth matrix, count and value waterfall, unresolved differences, owners, and completion proof |

## Final Checks

- No incompatible platform, entity, window, conversion, customer, revenue, or cohort totals are summed.
- Unknown, direct, consent-denied, deleted, unmatched, and immature states remain visible.
- No signup, lead, install, order, booking, payment, revenue, or contribution state is substituted for another.
- No attribution result is labeled causal lift, incremental growth, or budget authority.
- No universal weight, benchmark, score, forecast, provider capability, or legal conclusion is invented.
- No sensitive data, named journey, live access, export, configuration change, routing, publishing, spending, deployment, or result claim appears.
