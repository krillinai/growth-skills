## Required Order

Return:

1. mode, benchmark ID and version, decision, owner, focal unit, customer and business model, market, period, cutoff, review, expiry, privacy, and external-action boundary;
2. focal metric contract, actual or source state, baseline relationship, and decision use;
3. reference-class definitions, inclusion, exclusion, membership timing, missing units, and sensitivity alternatives;
4. source and lineage ledger with source type, original dataset, access, license, freshness, revisions, dependencies, conflicts, and evidence states;
5. population, sampling, selection, nonresponse, survivorship, publication, commercial, coverage, and missingness audit;
6. compatibility and normalization record across definitions, entities, periods, maturity, markets, currencies, accounting, topology, and source types;
7. focal and reference distributions, counts, denominators, uncertainty, percentiles or ranges where supported, sensitivity, and one verdict per comparison;
8. interpretation, transfer limits, counterevidence, causal boundary, target and forecast boundary, specialist handoffs, approval-ready external actions, pinned sources, and completion record.
