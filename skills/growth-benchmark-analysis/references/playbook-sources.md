## Source Rules

- Cite exact pinned sections and preserve the Playbook commit.
- Keep Playbook concepts separate from direct peer, company, metric, market, source, target, and causal evidence.
- Record focal and reference entity, population, product, model, metric, source type, market, period, maturity, currency, accounting, source, version, evidence state, limitation, and transfer boundary.
- Do not turn a framework, case, expert statement, vendor report, survey, percentile, repeated citation, or public page into a representative distribution, private fact, target, cause, implementation, or universal standard.
- Use current direct sources and accountable specialists for volatile provider, platform, market, benchmark, rights, legal, regulatory, privacy, tax, accounting, and security claims.
- Record a newer Playbook commit explicitly rather than silently mixing versions.
