# Directory Selection And Submission Pack

## Selection Test

Assess each directory on:

- customer, buyer, partner, or ecosystem audience and intent;
- entity and category fit;
- eligibility and verification feasibility;
- profile usefulness and destination continuity;
- trust, moderation, review integrity, and provenance;
- qualified visibility or demand evidence;
- data, brand, content, and review rights;
- paid placement, sponsorship, ranking, and disclosure;
- maintenance, ownership, duplicate, correction, and exit;
- full preparation, verification, subscription, advertising, support, and update cost;
- downstream value, attribution, dependency, and evidence time.

Assign `prepare`, `update`, `claim after authorization`, `merge`, `remove after authorization`, `monitor`, `defer`, `reject`, or `needs evidence`.

## Submission Pack

Return one pack per entity, directory, market, language, and listing version:

```text
directory and current requirement source
entity ID, owner, market, language, locale, and category
field name, exact value, source, evidence state, approval, and limit
short and long description variants with claim ledger
canonical destination and approved tracking parameters
logo, image, screenshot, and rights record
verification method and authorized owner
paid or sponsored state and disclosure
duplicate, existing listing, and ownership status
review checklist, unresolved fields, and blockers
submission handoff, confirmation evidence, update, correction, and retirement
```

Do not generate platform credentials, personal contact data, fake addresses, virtual branches, false categories, placeholder certifications, fabricated customers, or unapproved assets.

## URLs And Tracking

Prefer a stable canonical destination that continues the listing promise. Add approved tracking parameters only when the directory permits them and analytics ownership is clear. Record canonical, tracking version, redirect, locale, app or product state, expiry, and fallback.

Do not replace a canonical public URL with a campaign URL that may expire, leak private identifiers, create duplicate indexable pages, or break customer trust. Attribution parameters do not make traffic incremental.

## Quality Review

Check entity, spelling, category, facts, claims, proof, dates, price, availability, URLs, redirects, images, rights, accessibility, language, locale, disclosure, privacy, verification, duplicate, ownership, contact handoff, and mobile destination. Compare rendered public output with the approved pack after separately authorized publication.
