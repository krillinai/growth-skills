## Attribution Rules

- Describe the HubSpot Sales essay as Balfour's first-person retrospective, with approximate figures and a 2014-2016 scope.
- Describe LinkedIn and Superhuman examples as external analyses, not internal operating accounts.
- Do not attribute company outcomes to one person or framework.
- Do not copy long passages. Summarize the method and link the source.
- Do not imply endorsement, affiliation, private access, or current company facts.
- When public frameworks conflict with the user's first-party evidence, preserve the evidence and lower confidence in the framework-based inference.
