# Pricing Contract

Freeze this contract before researching, comparing, designing, validating, or migrating prices and packages. Split the work whenever one row cannot describe every customer, product, market, package, price, contract, or decision being combined.

## Decision Header

| Field | Required definition |
| --- | --- |
| Mode and decision | `audit`, `research`, `architecture`, or `migration`; owner, deadline, threshold, and actions not authorized |
| Customer and roles | Beneficiary, user, champion, administrator, buyer, approver, procurement, finance, payer, account, and contract hierarchy |
| Job and value | Trigger, current alternative, capability, outcome, evidence, urgency, budget, switching cost, risk, and willingness hypotheses |
| Segment | Observable inclusion and exclusion rules, product use, buying process, value, cost, and path differences |
| Product and offer | Product, version, package, entitlement, limit, service, add-on, price metric, and availability |
| Commercial terms | Price, currency, billing, commitment, included use, overage, minimum, maximum, discount, renewal, cancellation, refund, tax, invoice, and payment |
| Evidence and time | Source cutoff, research population and field dates, cohort, horizon, maturity, uncertainty, version, and review date |
| Market | Geography, language, locale, legal entity, channel, app distribution, procurement, data, and local owner |
| Action | Local analysis allowed now and separately authorized research, experiment, migration, publication, or production action |

## Participant And Economic Entity Map

Keep beneficiary, user, seat, workspace, account, champion, administrator, buyer, approver, procurement, legal, security, finance, payer, contract, subscription, order, transaction, buyer, seller, channel partner, and legal customer distinct. Define who receives, recognizes, evaluates, approves, pays for, implements, administers, renews, expands, downgrades, and bears risk for value.

Record hierarchy, shared and multiple roles, split budgets, multiple products, multiple contracts, transferred accounts, channel resellers, marketplace sides, and unresolved identity. A user's desire does not establish payer willingness, authority, budget, procurement feasibility, or company economics.

## Value And Alternative Contract

Define:

- customer job, trigger, current alternative, workaround, and doing-nothing path;
- differentiated capability, operational consequence, outcome, proof, time to value, natural frequency, and realized value evidence;
- beneficiary, buyer, and payer value, risk, uncertainty, budget, authority, and switching or implementation cost;
- best-fit and excluded conditions, market and product version, and counterevidence;
- whether value is stated, expected, observed, attributed, incremental, forecast, or unavailable.

Do not convert time saved, cost avoided, revenue enabled, risk reduced, activity, usage, or a feature into money without an attributable bridge and uncertainty. Route positioning claims and alternatives to `positioning` when they require a full positioning decision.

## Offer And Commercial Contract

For each current or proposed offer record product, package, core value, capabilities, entitlements, limits, service level, add-ons, compatibility, upgrade and downgrade, price metric, billing unit, list price, realized-price basis, currency, commitment, included use, overage, minimum, maximum, trial, free period, credit, discount, tax, invoice, payment, renewal, cancellation, refund, effective date, and eligible customer states.

Keep published, quoted, negotiated, contracted, billed, collected, recognized, and realized values separate. Record nonstandard terms, services, partner share, concessions, credits, free periods, and exception authority.

## Evidence Ledger

| ID | Claim or value | Participant and segment | Product, offer, market, date | Source and lineage | State | Coverage, uncertainty, limitation, next check |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Use only `verified`, `inferred`, `unavailable`, or `not applicable` for evidence-bearing rows. An attributable unsupported statement is a `reported signal`, not a fifth evidence state. Preserve zero, missing, inaccessible, stale, selected, disputed, censored, suppressed, forecast, target, benchmark, and not-applicable states.

## Compatibility Gate

Before combining or comparing, align participant and economic entity, segment, job, alternative, product and version, package, entitlement, price metric, price basis, currency, billing, commitment, contract, market, channel, cohort, time, source population, method, uncertainty, and maturity. If a bridge changes meaning, keep separate views.

Version contracts, sources, research instruments, stimuli, transformations, package maps, prices, terms, scenarios, overrides, migration states, outputs, decisions, and actualization. Preserve historical versions and explain revisions.
