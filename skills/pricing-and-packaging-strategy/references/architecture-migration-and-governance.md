# Architecture, Migration, And Governance

A price is one field inside a commercial system. Specify the full system, validate it against customer and business value, and migrate only through separately authorized controls.

## Commercial Architecture

For each package and eligible segment define:

- list and target realized price, currency, market, and effective dates;
- billing frequency, commitment, included use, overage, minimum, maximum, ramp, and true-up;
- trial, free period, pilot, credit, subsidy, and expiration;
- discount reason, give-get, authority, floor, duration, renewal, and exception;
- contract, order, invoice, tax, payment, collection, refund, cancellation, and dispute;
- service, support, implementation, partner, channel, and sales commitments;
- upgrade, downgrade, expansion, contraction, migration, grandfathering, and exit;
- metering, entitlement, billing, finance, CRM, data, reporting, and audit ownership.

Keep list, quoted, negotiated, contracted, billed, collected, recognized, and realized price separate. Reconcile free periods, discounts, credits, partner share, services, concessions, refunds, and nonstandard terms. Do not call price leakage until legitimate segment, commitment, volume, service, risk, and channel differences are considered.

## Price Levels And Scenarios

Use attributable compatible evidence to return a range or named scenarios, not a false optimum. For each scenario show customer and payer value hypothesis, package, metric, price, terms, eligible population, volume and conversion assumptions, retained use, churn, refund, expansion, service cost, contribution, cash, capacity, uncertainty, and strongest counterevidence.

Consume compatible outputs from `unit-economics-analysis`; do not recompute CAC, LTV, payback, or cash from partial pricing inputs. Preserve actual, estimate, forecast, scenario, target, plan, and benchmark. Never backsolve missing evidence to reach leadership revenue, margin, or ratio targets.

## Discount And Exception Governance

Record request, reason, customer condition, package, term, volume, commitment, value exchange, list and realized price, margin and cash effect, approver, evidence, expiry, renewal, fairness, sales incentive, channel conflict, precedent, and review.

Discounts can exchange uncertainty, volume, duration, payment timing, service scope, reference participation, or another legitimate term. They can also train delay, hide weak value, distort compensation, create unfairness, or damage renewal. Evaluate incremental conversion only with causal evidence. Do not remove existing exceptions or change contracts from an audit.

## Validation Gate

Every proposed architecture includes:

- hypotheses and compatible evidence;
- customer comprehension, value, trust, fairness, and control;
- activation, purchase, retained use, churn, refund, support, renewal, and expansion;
- contribution, cash, payback, service capacity, sales and implementation constraints;
- eligible and excluded populations, exposure, cohort, maturity, and data quality;
- research or experiment owner, decision rule, stop rule, correction, rollback, and expiry.

Short-term conversion is not validation. Keep preference, stated willingness, observed purchase, attributed change, incremental effect, forecast, and causal conclusion separate.

## Migration State Machine

Map:

```text
current offer and customer state
-> eligible target offer
-> notice, review, consent, and support readiness
-> entitlement, metering, billing, invoice, and payment readiness
-> bounded exposure cohort
-> activation, purchase, retained value, economics, and trust review
-> expand, hold, correct, roll back, grandfather, or retire
```

Define existing and new customers; trials, free plans, active subscriptions, contracts, renewals, delinquent accounts, discounts, exceptions, channel and partner customers, app-store customers, and unresolved states. State effective date, notice, applicable review, grandfathering, opt-in or opt-out where relevant, upgrade and downgrade, credits, proration, refunds, data and entitlement continuity, and customer support.

Use staged rollout only when measurement, customer protection, correction, and rollback are operationally ready. Preserve original and target package versions and cohort labels. Do not rewrite history or force an ineligible customer into the target state.

## Privacy And Fairness

Prefer aggregate, permissioned, redacted, pseudonymous, and small-cell-suppressed research and commercial evidence. Record purpose, authority, consent, access, lineage, retention, deletion, correction, fairness review, audit, incident, and retirement.

Do not use protected traits, health or financial vulnerability, exact location, private messages, employee notes, payment credentials, device graphs, or opaque willingness scores to personalize prices or discounts. Assess consistency and outcome fairness without turning protected traits into treatment rules. Do not use deception, hidden scarcity, difficult cancellation, or coercive feature loss.

## Market Transfer

Revalidate customer and payer roles, job, alternatives, value, package, metric, price, currency, billing, commitment, contract, discount, payment, tax, invoice, refund, channel, app distribution, procurement, data, research validity, economics, fairness, migration, source access, and owner for every market.

Translation and currency conversion do not validate transfer. For mainland China, use current direct sources and locally accountable review for time-sensitive platform, payment, app-store, invoicing, data, advertising, and provider conditions. Do not provide legal, tax, accounting, regulatory, or provider conclusions.

## Specialist Routing

Route overall commercial-system diagnosis to `monetization`, customer evidence to `customer-research`, segments to `icp-segmentation`, alternatives and value framing to `positioning`, causal design and readout to `experiment-design`, unit economics to `unit-economics-analysis`, LTV to `ltv-analysis`, retained-value mechanisms to `retention`, copy to `copywriting`, sales assets to `sales-enablement`, lifecycle communication to `lifecycle-marketing`, and launch sequencing to `product-launch-strategy`. Preserve entity, segment, market, offer version, cohort, terms, evidence state, uncertainty, and owner in every handoff.

## Decision States And Actions

Use `not comparable`, `evidence required`, `research ready`, `architecture candidate`, `bounded validation candidate`, `migration readiness review`, `hold`, `refresh`, or `retire` unless an approved vocabulary is supplied. These are not operating approvals.

Return local research plans, architecture specifications, scenario comparisons, migration plans, and approval-ready handoffs only. Do not contact participants, access systems, change commercial state, launch tests, publish, send, spend, deploy, or claim results without separate authorization.
