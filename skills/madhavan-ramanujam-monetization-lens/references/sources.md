## Attribution Rules

- Prefer direct interviews and talks over third-party summaries.
- Treat client examples and reported company counts as practitioner accounts.
- Do not infer a precise price, demand curve, or financial forecast from a framework.
- Do not imply endorsement, affiliation, private access, or current client facts.
- Preserve customer, product, cost, legal, tax, and accounting evidence when it conflicts with the framework.
