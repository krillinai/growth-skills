# Customer Health Modeling Output Contract

## Required Order

Return:

1. mode, model ID and version, state, decision, customer entity, outcome, prediction time, horizon, population, product, market, owners, review, expiry, privacy and external actions;
2. customer, role, account, workspace, team, legal entity, product, contract, subscription, invoice, opportunity and renewal hierarchy;
3. lifecycle, value, reliability, implementation, support, relationship, commercial, decision and outcome state contracts;
4. source, availability, data-vintage, label, maturity, censoring, missingness, correction and evidence manifest;
5. feature, aggregation, leakage, access, coverage, human-signal, privacy and fairness audit;
6. dimensional ledger or model, baselines, training population, algorithm, parameters, calibration, uncertainty and versions;
7. out-of-time validation, threshold and capacity analysis, reason codes, suppressions, abstention, override, correction and appeal;
8. monitoring, drift, feedback, market transfer, specialist and approval-ready handoffs, pinned sources, residual obligations and completion.
