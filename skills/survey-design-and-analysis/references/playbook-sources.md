## Source Rules

- Cite exact pinned sections and preserve the Playbook commit.
- Keep Playbook concepts separate from direct survey, respondent, sample, market, behavioral, and operating evidence.
- Record construct, item, unit, population, frame, sample, role, product, market, language, instrument, field period, source, version, evidence state, limitation, and transfer boundary.
- Do not turn a framework, case, expert statement, benchmark, questionnaire, score, threshold, article, or repeated practice into a valid instrument, representative result, cause, decision, implementation, or universal pattern.
- Use current direct sources and accountable specialists for volatile platform, provider, benchmark, market, rights, legal, regulatory, privacy, and security claims.
- Record a newer Playbook commit explicitly rather than silently mixing versions.
