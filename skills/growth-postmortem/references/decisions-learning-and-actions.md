# Decisions, Learning, And Actions

## Evaluate Quality Without Outcome Bias

| Layer | Review questions | Do not infer |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Outcome | Did mature customer, business, economic, and guardrail states improve, decline, remain flat, or stay unavailable? | that the team caused all movement |
| Decision | Were alternatives, evidence, uncertainty, authority, reversibility, downside, and rules appropriate ex ante? | quality from outcome alone |
| Evidence | Were definitions, coverage, maturity, validity, counterevidence, and sources adequate? | confidence from data volume |
| Execution | Was the authorized plan delivered, adapted, verified, and stopped or rolled back correctly? | impact from task completion |
| Coordination | Were ownership, handoffs, dependencies, escalation, and communication effective? | consensus from attendance |
| System | Did data, experimentation, infrastructure, process, resilience, and maintenance support valid decisions? | maturity from tool count |
| Protection | Did customer, employee, accessibility, privacy, security, fairness, and trust controls hold? | that upside compensates for harm |
| Learning | Did evidence change a decision, artifact, system, capability, or retirement rule? | adoption from publication |

Avoid composite scores. Preserve tradeoffs, external conditions, variance, luck, and unknown causal contribution.

## Build A Learning Contract

For each proposed learning record:

- observation and exact evidence;
- interpretation and alternatives;
- causal or noncausal status;
- supporting and contradictory evidence;
- customer, product, market, mechanism, metric, period, and maturity boundary;
- decision changed or preserved;
- artifact, system, capability, portfolio, interface, maintenance, or retirement target;
- owner, transfer condition, failure signal, expiry, and review.

Classify it as `retain`, `revise`, `invalidate`, `unresolved`, or `not transferable`. A negative, flat, harmful, or inconclusive outcome can produce valuable learning when it changes a real decision. A document with no downstream change is an observation archive, not adopted learning.

## Select A Follow-Up Type

| Type | Use |
| --- | --- |
| `preserve` | Keep a sound choice, control, capability, or practice and its boundary |
| `correct` | Repair an attributable data, product, customer, process, or record defect |
| `prevent` | Reduce recurrence through a verified design or control change |
| `detect` | Improve monitoring, quality checks, alerts, or review gates |
| `mitigate` | Bound customer, system, economic, or operating exposure |
| `investigate` | Resolve a discriminating evidence gap |
| `experiment` | Test a causal mechanism under a valid design |
| `reuse` | Apply a supported artifact or capability inside an explicit transfer boundary |
| `standardize` | Make stable repeated practice available with ownership and exceptions |
| `retire` | Stop an invalid, harmful, obsolete, duplicative, or unowned practice or asset |
| `route` | Hand specialist work to the accountable workflow |

Do not make every finding an action. Some findings preserve a choice or remain unresolved.

## Govern The Action Ledger

For every action record decision, type, owner, accepted scope, artifact or operating state, evidence, dependencies, capacity, budget or approval, due or maturity date, priority, guardrails, completion proof, stop, rollback, escalation, expiry, and residual obligations.

Use action states `proposed`, `accepted`, `active`, `blocked`, `complete`, `cancelled`, `superseded`, or `expired`. Status is not completion proof. Require a versioned artifact, verified operating state, mature outcome, or accountable decision record as appropriate.

Avoid actions such as “communicate better,” “move faster,” or “test more” without a bounded decision and observable state. Assign one accountable owner, not everyone.

## Transfer Learning Carefully

Before reuse, revalidate customer, product, market, channel, platform, metric, mechanism, evidence maturity, scale, economics, capacity, risk, and owner. Separate a reusable principle from the original tactic and conditions.

Track where the learning is proposed, accepted, implemented, verified, contradicted, superseded, or expired. Do not infer adoption or effect from training attendance, publication, template update, or tool availability.

## Close The Loop

Hand accepted decisions and open actions to `growth-operating-review` with source postmortem version, owner, evidence, dependency, date, guardrail, proof, escalation, and expiry. Hand changed planning assumptions to `growth-planning-cycle`, initiative changes to `growth-initiative-planning`, and system or organization changes to their owning Skills.

A postmortem becomes `closed` only when required review decisions and follow-up obligations have completion, supersession, cancellation, or expiry records. Business improvement may remain unavailable and must not be claimed.
