# Google Ads Evidence And Boundaries

## Evidence States

Use `verified`, `reported signal`, `inferred`, `unavailable`, or `not applicable` for each material claim.

- `verified`: a supplied attributable source supports the statement within compatible scope and date;
- `reported signal`: a stakeholder or source reports a condition not independently verified;
- `inferred`: a transparent interpretation whose assumptions and alternatives remain visible;
- `unavailable`: needed evidence was not supplied, accessible, compatible, mature, or trustworthy enough;
- `not applicable`: the question does not apply to the declared decision.

Missing private data is not failure. Request the smallest privacy-safe evidence that can change the decision.

## Claim Controls

- Google-attributed conversion and value do not establish incrementality, contribution, payback, or retention.
- A search term establishes reported query text, not identity, complete intent, or representativeness.
- A primary conversion action does not establish that the action is qualified or economically valuable.
- Optimization score, recommendation, quality score, ad strength, policy status, and asset label establish the recorded platform signal only.
- A CPA, value-per-cost, conversion, or impression-share movement does not establish its cause.
- Automated bidding or serving does not make its exact decision logic inspectable from exports.
- A screenshot verifies only the visible surface, scope, and capture time.
- External benchmarks require compatible market, channel type, goal, definition, window, maturity, and economics before use.

Preserve counterevidence and unresolved discrepancies. Cite local files, row ranges, captures, and dates.

## Unsupported Precision

Never invent a universal campaign or ad-group structure, keyword or negative count, match-type mix, asset quantity, quality-score target, impression-share target, budget, bid, CPA, ROAS, payback, scaling rule, stopping rule, attribution window, conversion delay, audience size, or incrementality estimate.

When economics are incomplete, return the blocked decision, scenario formula, missing inputs, and evidence plan rather than a temporary platform threshold.

## Read-Only And Privacy Boundary

This Skill may read supplied local files, validate a local bundle, calculate descriptive measures from compatible rows, compare dated ad, query, asset, product, destination, measurement, and business evidence, and write local findings or experiment plans.

It must not access Google accounts or ask for credentials; call a live API; create, edit, publish, pause, or delete campaigns or related entities; change bids, budgets, goals, networks, feeds, placements, audiences, exclusions, tracking, or destinations; upload Customer Match or conversion data; or claim those actions occurred.

Do not process direct identifiers, credentials, developer tokens, OAuth secrets, Customer Match lists, payment records, raw offline-conversion identifiers, or click identifiers linked to people. Stop using the affected artifact without reproducing sensitive values and request a redacted or aggregated replacement.

An execution request uses `out-of-scope`. Preserve supported diagnosis and list the separate authorization, capability, policy, privacy, approval, monitoring, rollback, and audit-log controls required. Do not provide instructions designed to bypass the boundary.
