## Source Rules

- Cite the exact pinned page and section used.
- Keep Playbook concepts and cases separate from organization-specific facts and direct evidence.
- Preserve source date, version, customer, product, market, method, maturity, authority, and transfer limit.
- Do not use a framework, case, analogy, expert statement, or lack of incidents as proof of risk, control effectiveness, acceptance, compliance, or safety.
- Use current direct sources and accountable specialists for changing legal, regulatory, security, privacy, provider, platform, market, vendor, contract, insurance, and operating conditions.
- If a newer Playbook version is used, record its commit and do not silently mix versions.
