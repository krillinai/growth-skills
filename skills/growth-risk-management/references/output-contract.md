## Required Order

Return:

1. mode, portfolio ID and version, state, decisions and intended uses, owners and authorities, scope, products, customers, markets, initiatives, systems, periods, cutoff, maturity, access, privacy, review, expiry, and external-action boundary;
2. source, evidence, assumption, uncertainty, contradiction, definition, metric, version, access, and correction ledger;
3. taxonomy and risk register with state, objective, cause, uncertain event, consequence, affected population, horizon, evidence, controls, exposure, owner, and decision;
4. capacity, appetite, tolerance, limits, guardrails, triggers, stop rules, baselines, current exposure, breaches, exceptions, authority, review, and expiry;
5. shared dependencies, common causes, correlations, cascades, reinforcement, offsets, competition, duplicates, concentrations, portfolio capacity, scenarios, stress, and sensitivity;
6. preventive, detective, corrective, recovery, directive, and compensating controls with design, approval, implementation, operation, coverage, tests, exceptions, failures, overrides, effectiveness, and residual exposure;
7. avoid, reduce, transfer, share, accept, monitor, investigate, pause, stop, exit, and remediate options with evidence, dependencies, cost, capacity, tradeoffs, obligations, verification, and decision states;
8. monitoring, indicators, maturity, thresholds, blind spots, alerts, near misses, breaches, incidents, losses, escalation, containment, recovery, closure, correction, refresh, and supersession;
9. governance, market, privacy, fairness, sensitive-data, acceptance, segregation-of-duties, specialist-handoff, and downstream-consumer records;
10. pinned Growth Playbook sources, approval-ready external actions, and completion record.
