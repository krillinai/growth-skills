# Growth Operating Review Output Contract

## Required Order

Return:

1. review ID, version, mode, cadence, period, as-of date, evidence cutoff, owner, audience, authority, scope, market, prior versions, target decisions, and external-action boundary;
2. source and metric-contract ledger with evidence states, freshness, maturity, quality, revisions, contradictions, and unavailable inputs;
3. executive decision view: material change, customer and business effect, constraint, risk, decision, owner, and date;
4. customer value, lifecycle, acquisition, growth accounting, economic, cash, forecast, experiment, portfolio, system, and risk views that are material;
5. anomaly and hypothesis register with causal limits and next discriminating evidence;
6. decision register, commitment ledger, escalations, expiry, and changes from the prior review;
7. specialist handoffs, relevant pinned Growth Playbook sources, approval-ready external actions, and completion record.

## Metric Health Register

| Metric | Contract and source | Current evidence | Comparison | Absolute and relative change | Freshness and maturity | Quality, revision, contradiction | Decision use and owner |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Do not compare incompatible entities, windows, cohorts, markets, currencies, or accounting bases. Immature is unavailable, not zero.

## Executive Decision Register

| Decision | Customer and business context | Alternatives | Evidence and contradiction | Risk and guardrail | State and rationale | Owner / approver | Date / expiry / trigger |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Use `decide`, `continue`, `investigate`, `escalate`, `defer`, `stop`, or `route`. A decision record is not authorization or execution.

## Reconciliation Register

| View | Opening or baseline | Positive movements | Negative movements | Adjustments | Closing or current | Mix and maturity | Reconciliation status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Keep customer counts, recurring value, revenue, contribution, and cash in separate compatible views.

## Anomaly And Hypothesis Register

| Observation | Data-quality state | Affected scope and value | Candidate mechanism | Supporting / contradictory / missing evidence | Next evidence | Owner and urgency | Response boundary |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Do not claim cause from movement, correlation, attribution, or timing alone.

## Experiment And Portfolio Register

| Item | Decision or thesis | Evidence and maturity | Customer and economic outcome | Capacity and dependency | Risk and guardrail | Retain / revise / accelerate / cap / pause / graduate / retire | Owner and review |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Do not select only wins, add uplifts, or mark every initiative on track.

## Commitment And Handoff Register

| Commitment or workstream | Expected artifact or outcome | Evidence required | Owner | Date | Dependency | State / escalation / expiry | Permission boundary |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

`In progress` is not completion proof. Do not imply a handoff or action has executed.

## Completion Gate

Confirm that review ID, version, cadence, period, as-of date, evidence cutoff, owner, audience, authority, scope, market, prior decisions, strategy and forecast versions, sources, metric contracts, entities, eligibility, numerators, denominators, windows, cohorts, maturity, freshness, revisions, actuals, targets, forecasts, comparisons, customer value, lifecycle, acquisition, growth accounting, revenue, contribution, cash, experiments, anomalies, hypotheses, causal boundaries, initiatives, capacity, risks, guardrails, decisions, commitments, approvers, dates, expiry, escalation, market transfer, privacy, handoffs, pinned sources, and external actions are explicit; immature was not zero; status was not impact; variance was not cause; attribution was not incrementality; experiment uplifts were not added; customers and value were not double counted; forecasts and history were not rewritten; unknowns, approvals, execution, and results were not invented; and no external action occurred.
