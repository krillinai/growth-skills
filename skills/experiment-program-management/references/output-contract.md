# Experiment Program Management Output Contract

## Required Order

Return:

1. mode, program ID and version, state, purpose, owners, reviewers, approvers, scope, customers, products, markets, periods, horizons, evidence cutoff, systems, review, expiry, privacy, and external-action boundary;
2. complete intake and inventory with decisions, uncertainties, mechanisms, evidence, design families, states, sources, maturity, exclusions, and missing inputs;
3. portfolio classes and selection decisions with customer and strategic value, evidence, traffic, capacity, sensitivity, dependencies, interference, risk, opportunity cost, and expiry;
4. time-phased traffic, population, surface, holdout, engineering, data, analysis, specialist-review, support, and decision-attention reconciliation;
5. concurrency, mutual exclusion, interaction, contamination, spillover, carryover, novelty, and concurrent-change register;
6. preflight quality gates, risk tiers, exceptions, incidents, corrections, containment, validity, stop, rollback, and residual-risk ledger;
7. maturity, readout, recommendation, decision, follow-through, completion-proof, mature-outcome, proxy, long-term-validation, and reopen registers;
8. learning reuse, disaggregated health, decision rights, market and privacy boundaries, handoffs, pinned sources, approval-ready external actions, and completion record.
