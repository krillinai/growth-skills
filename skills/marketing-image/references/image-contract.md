# Marketing Image Contract

## Intake

Capture independently:

- decision, owner, approver, asset ID, status, deadline, budget or capacity, and allowed action;
- product, edition, feature, offer, price, market, language, locale, destination, and available customer experience;
- audience, job, situation, lifecycle or buying state, problem, objection, value pillar, angle, proof, CTA, and qualified outcome;
- image role, concept ID, composition ID, variant ID, source asset, parent version, and experiment;
- channel, platform, account, campaign, placement, device, format, current requirement source, dimensions, ratio, safe area, crop, file type, size, quality, and naming;
- exact copy, hierarchy, claim, statistic, price, disclaimer, source, proof, limitation, owner, approval, expiry, and prohibited wording;
- brand version, logo, colors, typography, layout, photography, illustration, icon, product UI, and prohibited elements;
- asset title or ID, creator, owner, license, model or property release, trademark, copyright, media, derivative, generative use, market, term, attribution, and evidence artifact;
- depicted person, identity, likeness, age, sensitive context, consent, role, endorsement, edit, synthetic state, and disclosure;
- accessibility purpose, surrounding content, alt-text requirement, text equivalent, contrast, motion or flashing if applicable, and review;
- tool or model, prompt, edit, seed or output ID where available, source hashes or locators, date, output manifest, and QA result;
- source artifact, owner, capture date, evidence state, counterevidence, limitation, and freshness.

Version when concept, message, source, rights, product, brand, copy, claim, market, placement, dimensions, or approval changes materially.

## Evidence And Rights

Use `verified`, `inferred`, `unavailable`, or `not applicable`. A `reported signal` is an attributable statement without inspectable support and has no evidence state.

Public availability, search results, social posts, screenshots, logos, customer pages, press coverage, stock previews, and model outputs do not establish ownership, license, endorsement, model release, derivative, paid-media, global, perpetual, training, or generative-use rights.

Missing rights block the affected use; they do not imply that the asset is free. Preserve license terms, market, channel, edit, derivative, attribution, exclusivity, expiration, and evidence.

## Product And Claim Truth

Use approved real product captures where inspectability matters. A conceptual or future-state mockup must be clearly labeled and cannot be presented as currently available. Preserve warnings, errors, limitations, required steps, and representative data.

For every visualized result or comparison, record population, method, source, period, unit, baseline, uncertainty, conditions, and counterevidence. Do not create fake analytics dashboards, reviews, messages, receipts, maps, news, certifications, or before-and-after proof.
