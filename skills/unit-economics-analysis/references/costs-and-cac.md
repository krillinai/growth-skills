# Costs And CAC

CAC is a contract, not one universal number. Name the numerator, denominator, timing, source, and causal status every time.

## Cost Ledger

Reconcile source costs into declared buckets:

- media, placement, marketplace, affiliate, sponsorship, and partner fees;
- sales compensation, commission, bonus, travel, enablement, and sales tools;
- agency, contractor, creative production, localization, data, and measurement;
- discounts, credits, referral rewards, trials, incentives, and acquisition subsidies;
- onboarding or implementation subsidies, fraud, chargebacks, refunds, and failed payments;
- acquisition operations, allocated labor, shared tools, and approved overhead.

Record amount, currency, period, cash date, accounting date, purpose, product, market, channel, campaign, cohort, direct or allocated status, allocation key, owner, source, and evidence state. Preserve unallocated and disputed costs.

## Denominator Contract

Define the acquired economic unit and qualification state. Keep lead, signup, activated user, converted payer, account, contract, order, and retained customer distinct. Use stable identities, deduplicate overlap, preserve unknown source, and state acquisition, attribution, sales-cycle, conversion, maturity, refund, and exclusion windows.

## CAC Variants

```text
CAC variant = eligible declared acquisition cost / compatible acquired economic units
marginal CAC = change in compatible cost / change in compatible acquired units over a declared band
incremental CAC = incremental eligible cost / incremental acquired units supported by causal evidence
```

Media, direct, sales, fully loaded, attributed, incremental, blended, channel, average, and marginal CAC answer different questions. Do not rename one as another. Do not treat a negative or unstable marginal denominator as a precise cost.

## Attribution And Incrementality

Platform, first-touch, last-touch, multi-touch, modeled, self-reported, and sales-source credit are operational attribution. They do not establish how many customers would not have arrived without spend. Preserve view-through, overlap, organic demand, offline, partner, direct, and unknown states.

Route attribution-model work to `attribution-analysis` and causal designs to `experiment-design`. Report incremental CAC only from a credible compatible causal estimate with uncertainty.

## Marginal Returns And Saturation

Compare spend bands with eligible acquired units, retained quality, contribution, cash, uncertainty, and capacity. Model auction pressure, audience exhaustion, sales and support limits, channel interaction, fraud, concentration, and time to mature. Historical average CAC is not the next-dollar response curve.

Do not automatically move spend. Return evidence-supported bands, reversal points, constraints, and separately authorized decision handoffs.
