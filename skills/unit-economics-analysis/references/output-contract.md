Return Markdown unless the user requests another local format. Keep public facts, supplied values, observed actuals, inferences, forecasts, scenarios, targets, benchmarks, attributed outputs, incremental estimates, unavailable evidence, causal claims, and operating approvals separate.

State mode, decision, owner, customer and payer entity, acquisition cohort and event, product, offer, market, channel, cost and value bases, observed and forecast horizons, source cutoff, model version, review date, non-goals, and external-action boundary.

## 5. Value And Payback Bridge

Show booked, billed, collected, refunded, recognized revenue, gross profit, contribution before acquisition, contribution after acquisition, and cash. Then return by period:

| Period | Original cohort | Active or payer state | Revenue | Contribution before acquisition | Acquisition and service cash | Net cash | Cumulative contribution | Cumulative net cash | Maturity |

State observed accounting payback, observed cash payback, or no observed payback. Label every forecast date conditional and expose assumptions and uncertainty.

## 6. LTV Compatibility And Portfolio

Document LTV entity, cohort, value basis, acquisition-cost inclusion, horizon, market, currency, version, observed and forecast split, calibration, and uncertainty. Do not return LTV:CAC until compatibility passes.

Compare local cohorts, spend bands, products, channels, segments, markets, freemium paths, or marketplace sides with absolute values, weights, mix, overlap, shared costs, cannibalization, saturation, capacity, concentration, and risk before any aggregate.

## 7. Scenarios And Decisions

Return observed values, bounded scenarios or intervals, one-way and coherent sensitivity, forecast calibration, thresholds, triggers, counterevidence, decision states, smallest next evidence, owner, expiry, and review date. Preserve universal or weakly matched benchmarks as context, not policy.

## 8. Governance And Completion

List privacy and fairness controls, market-transfer gaps, source and model refresh, specialist handoffs, approval-ready external actions, monitoring, rollback, and retirement. Confirm pinned Playbook sources, compatibility checks, evidence states, no fabricated precision or causality, and no unauthorized access, export, contact, publication, system change, spend, deployment, customer treatment, or result claim.
