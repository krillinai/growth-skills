## 6. Portfolio And Sensitivity

Show compatible cohort, product, channel, segment, market, spend-band, and scenario views; absolute costs and values; weights, mix, concentration, saturation, capacity, interactions, reported and constant currency, sensitivities, rank reversals, and unresolved alternatives.

## 7. Decisions And Governance

Return observations, counterevidence, thresholds, risks, smallest discriminating evidence, specialist handoffs, owners, refresh and retirement triggers, privacy controls, market-transfer limits, and approval-ready external actions.

## 8. Completion Record

List pinned Playbook sources, unavailable evidence, unresolved assumptions, incompatibilities, next checks, review dates, and completion proof. Confirm attributed did not become incremental; average did not become marginal; revenue did not become contribution or cash; forecast value did not become observed; immature payback did not become realized; incompatible ratios were not calculated; and no system or customer state changed.
