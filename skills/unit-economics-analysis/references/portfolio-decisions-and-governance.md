# Portfolio Decisions And Governance

Build compatible local economics before portfolio totals. A portfolio model informs an owner; it does not execute the decision.

## Portfolio Views

For each cohort, product, channel, segment, market, sales motion, and spend band show:

- absolute cost, acquired and retained units, value, contribution, and cash;
- CAC variant, maturity, horizon, LTV handoff, payback state, and uncertainty;
- base size, weight, mix shift, concentration, saturation, capacity, interaction, and residual;
- attributed, predicted, and incremental states;
- reported and constant currency where relevant.

Do not average ratios or hide segment reversals. Separate small high-growth cohorts, mature portfolios, enterprise sales cycles, consumer purchases, freemium conversion, and marketplace sides.

## Decision Contract

State decision, owner, cadence, deadline, reversibility, cost of over- and underinvestment, threshold, uncertainty, cash and capacity guardrails, customer and trust effects, fallback, approval, actualization date, and retirement condition.

Use recommendations such as `review`, `validate`, `pilot`, `cap`, `hold`, `fallback`, or `stop candidate`. These labels do not change budgets, bids, prices, discounts, routing, campaigns, or products.

## Privacy And Fairness

Use minimum necessary, aggregate, redacted, pseudonymous, consented, and small-cell-suppressed inputs. Define purpose, authority, access, retention, deletion, correction, audit, and incident ownership.

Do not request payment credentials, protected traits, health or financial details, private messages, exact locations, complete device graphs, personal notes, or raw identities when cohorts suffice. Do not rank people by predicted value or use unit economics to route prices or offers without a separately justified and governed policy. Audit error and customer effects across relevant groups without making protected traits treatment rules.

## Market Transfer And China

Revalidate customer, payer, product, price, channel, sales cycle, platform, payment, tax, invoice, refund, currency, attribution, identity, consent, data, storage, transfer, acquisition cost, retention, margin, servicing, cash, model, and ownership by market.

For mainland China, use current direct sources and locally accountable review for time-sensitive platform, payment, invoice, tax, advertising, data, and regulatory conditions. Translation and currency conversion do not validate transferred economics. Do not provide legal, tax, accounting, regulatory, or provider conclusions.

## Specialist Routing And Cadence

Route LTV to `ltv-analysis`, metrics to `growth-metrics-design`, cohorts to `cohort-analysis`, attribution to `attribution-analysis`, incrementality to `experiment-design`, allocation to `acquisition-strategy`, pricing and retained-value mechanisms to `monetization`, movement reconciliation to `growth-accounting`, forecasts to `growth-forecasting`, and finance controls to `revops-audit`.

Version source cutoff, cost allocation, customer definitions, value basis, models, assumptions, outputs, decisions, actuals, calibration, overrides, and retirement. Track missing costs, unresolved identity, maturity, forecast error, payback revisions, saturation, concentration, and stale decisions.
