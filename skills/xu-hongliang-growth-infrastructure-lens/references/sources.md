## Attribution Rules

- Treat operating details and reported figures as Xu's interview account unless independently verified.
- Keep practices tied to the products, regions, teams, and periods discussed.
- Do not attribute ByteDance outcomes to one person or system.
- Do not imply endorsement, private access, or current internal organization.
- Preserve first-party product evidence when it conflicts with the framework.
