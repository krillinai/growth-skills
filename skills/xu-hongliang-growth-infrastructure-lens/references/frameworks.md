# Framework Reference

## Shared Growth Platform

Inventory reusable capabilities such as forecasting, attribution, delivery, asset management, re-engagement, push, incentives, anti-abuse, and portfolio traffic. Define shared interfaces, ownership, service levels, permissions, product overrides, audit, and feedback. Centralize repeated capability, not every decision.

## Multi-Horizon Value System

Estimate value and ROI at several decision horizons. Segment by cohort, market, channel, product, and incentive exposure. Backtest predictions against mature outcomes, recalibrate drift, expose uncertainty, and keep loss, cash, and trust guardrails.

## Human-Governed Automation

```text
objective and constraints
-> data and prediction
-> automated allocation or delivery
-> outcome observation
-> calibration and audit
-> human exception and policy update
```

Humans retain responsibility for objectives, data quality, creative direction, exceptional events, approval, risk, and shutdown.

## Creative-Risk Production System

Map brief, generation, tagging, rights, review, delivery, performance feedback, incident handling, and reuse. Measure validated creative learning, not asset count. Add policy, brand, fraud, social, provenance, permission, and market-specific review layers.

## Segmented Incentive System

Define target segment, desired durable behavior, incentive type, duration, isolation, economics, and exit. Compare incrementality and post-incentive activation, retention, monetization, recommendation quality, cannibalization, and user trust.

## Anti-Abuse Architecture

Model attacker incentives, identity, device, payment, referral graph, velocity, collusion, and false-positive cost. Layer eligibility, limits, anomaly detection, review, appeals, recovery, and incident feedback. Risk control is part of product design.

## Cross-Product Allocation

Match portfolio traffic by user intent, product value, eligibility, marginal opportunity cost, cannibalization, and retained outcome. Use holdouts to estimate incrementality. Do not move users merely because inventory exists.

## Localized Growth System

Rebuild market priorities, channel mix, creative context, language, regulation, payments, devices, connectivity, fraud controls, value horizon, and operating ownership. Establish local evidence and decision rights rather than importing domestic benchmarks.

## Measurement and Calibration Audit

Review event quality, identity, attribution windows, incrementality, delayed outcomes, model error, drift, feedback loops, and missing counterfactuals. Match measurement certainty to evidence quality.
