## Attribution Rules

- Treat product and culture claims as founder and CEO accounts.
- Do not infer learning efficacy from engagement or company growth.
- Do not attribute Duolingo outcomes to one mechanic or person.
- Do not imply endorsement, private access, or current internal facts.
- Preserve first-party product evidence when it conflicts with the framework.
