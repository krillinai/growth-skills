## Source Rules

- Cite exact pinned sections and preserve the Playbook commit.
- Keep Playbook concepts separate from direct organization evidence and accountable authority.
- Record customer, participant, product, market, system, metric, period, maturity, limitation, and transfer boundary.
- Do not turn a framework, case, expert statement, translation, announcement, or repeated practice into readiness, adoption, causal proof, or universal policy.
- Use current direct sources and accountable specialists for volatile product, provider, market, price, rights, legal, regulatory, security, privacy, and platform claims.
- Record a newer Playbook commit explicitly rather than silently mixing versions.
