# Growth Change Management Output Contract

## Required Order

Return:

1. mode, change ID and version, record state, decision, owners, authority, scope, products, customers, participant roles, markets, systems, periods, cutoff, review, expiry, access, privacy, and external-action boundary;
2. source, version and request ledger, decomposition, parent-child changes, current and target states, exclusions, unresolved conflicts, and evidence coverage;
3. participant roles, direct and indirect impact, value, burden, permissions, authority, communication, enablement, support, feedback, adoption evidence, and protections;
4. dependencies, capacity, concurrent change load, sequencing, prerequisites, conflicts, blackout periods, buffers, opportunity cost, and residual obligations;
5. readiness gates, evidence, owners, blockers, exceptions, conditions, decisions, dissent, stop and rollback authority;
6. communication and enablement contracts with distinct supply, delivery, comprehension, practice, competence, behavior, adoption, correction, and retirement states;
7. migration map, eligibility, cohorts, trial, dual run, reconciliation, exceptions, cutover, guardrails, support, rollback, fallback, recovery, archive, and decommissioning;
8. rollout and adoption state chain, metric contracts, maturity, workflow value, workarounds, support, trust, accessibility, retention, economics, harm, outcomes, and causal limits;
9. version conflicts, market, privacy, fairness, dissent, specialist handoffs, pinned Growth Playbook sources, approval-ready external actions, and completion record.

## Completion Gate

Confirm change ID, version, mode, state, decision, authority, source lineage, evidence states, scope, exclusions, products, customers, roles, markets, systems, periods, current and target states, decomposition, impacts, dependencies, capacity, change load, readiness, blockers, conditions, communications, enablement, migration, cohorts, reconciliation, cutover, support, guardrails, stop, rollback, recovery, rollout, adoption, metrics, maturity, outcomes, causal limits, decommissioning, residual obligations, conflicts, privacy, fairness, dissent, specialist handoffs, pinned sources, external actions, and completion are explicit or unavailable with the missing basis recorded; unrelated changes were not combined; authority was not inferred; unsupported scores, roles, dates, phases, targets, actions, acknowledgements, adoption, and benefits were not populated; blockers were not averaged away; delivery and attendance did not become competence; enablement and exposure did not become adoption; activity and adoption did not become benefit; versions were not overwritten; old states were not retired without proof; sensitive data and dissent were protected; routed work was not claimed complete; and no external action occurred.
