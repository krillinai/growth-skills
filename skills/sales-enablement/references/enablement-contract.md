# Sales Enablement Contract

## Intake

Capture independently:

- decision, owner, approver, scope, motion, horizon, review cadence, budget or capacity, and allowed action;
- product, edition, module, service, bundle, package, price, discount, offer, contract, and effective version;
- customer, user, account, buying and implementation units; market, language, locale, industry, and route to market;
- champion, user, buyer, payer, approver, blocker, administrator, executive, security, legal, procurement, finance, implementation, partner, and seller roles;
- buying situation, trigger, job, urgency, current alternative, status quo, no-decision condition, evaluation criteria, risk, and next decision;
- best-fit, prerequisite, disqualification, exclusion, authority, readiness, service, capacity, economic, and implementation conditions;
- capability, consequence, differentiated value, proof, claim, limitation, counterevidence, objection, and prohibited wording;
- current seller process, buyer milestones, stage entry and exit, handoffs, required evidence, exceptions, and ownership;
- asset inventory, purpose, audience, stage, source, owner, version, approval, distribution, access, usage, feedback, freshness, and status;
- opportunity, account, buyer progression, commercial, implementation, activation, retention, support, training, and content evidence;
- privacy, confidentiality, rights, security, accessibility, localization, sharing, retention, and requested external action.

Version the contract when product, customer scope, motion, stage, offer, pricing, claim, proof, competitor, market, process, or approval changes materially. Do not merge incompatible versions into one play or performance view.

## Evidence States

Use:

- `verified`: directly supported by an attributable inspectable artifact;
- `inferred`: reasoned from named evidence, with the inference explicit;
- `unavailable`: decision-relevant but absent, inaccessible, declined, or stale;
- `not applicable`: outside the declared decision and scope;
- `reported signal`: attributable stakeholder statement without inspectable support; leave evidence state blank.

Public evidence can verify visible product pages, public claims, published prices, documentation, public competitor statements, and dated market artifacts. It cannot verify private buying motives, account state, authority, budget, deal progression, product realization, win or loss cause, sales behavior, economics, or enablement impact.

## Atomic Claim Record

Record:

```text
claim ID and exact wording
product, edition, package, audience, market, stage, and asset scope
claim type and source artifact
proof type, date, conditions, limitations, and counterevidence
owner, specialist reviewer, approval, expiry, and review trigger
allowed variants and prohibited stronger wording
```

Do not upgrade a customer report into an objective outcome, a benchmark into universal superiority, a certification into legal approval, a demo into production reliability, or a forecast into realized ROI.

## Privacy And Confidentiality

Prefer redacted or aggregate opportunity, call, usage, outcome, and performance evidence. Exclude raw contact data, credentials, private links, personal messages, sensitive traits, unrelated employee performance, and unnecessary account details.

Preserve supplied confidentiality, consent, allowed purpose, access, output audience, retention, and deletion. Do not move a private call quote, customer logo, deal detail, security response, or pricing term into a reusable asset without explicit scoped permission.
