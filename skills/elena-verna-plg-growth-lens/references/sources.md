## Attribution Rules

- Keep claims tied to the role, company, stage, and date represented by the source.
- Do not use reported company growth as proof that one method caused the outcome.
- Treat provocative talk titles as framing, not universal laws.
- Do not imply endorsement, private access, or current company facts.
- Preserve first-party product evidence when it conflicts with the framework.
