## Source Rules

- Cite exact pinned sections and preserve the Playbook commit.
- Keep Playbook concepts separate from direct customer, journey, system and market evidence.
- Record customer entity, role, job, product, market, channel, state, transition, metric, period, maturity, source, limitation and transfer boundary.
- Do not turn a framework, case, expert statement, presentation sequence, public page or repeated practice into a complete journey, private operating fact, cause, implementation or universal pattern.
- Use current direct sources and accountable specialists for volatile product, provider, platform, market, price, rights, legal, regulatory, privacy and security claims.
- Record a newer Playbook commit explicitly rather than silently mixing versions.
