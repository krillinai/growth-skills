# Output Contract

Use this order. Keep verified evidence, reported statements, customer attribution, inference, unavailable inputs, derivative claims, approvals, and causal conclusions visibly distinct.

## 1. Decision Header

State mode, decision, owner, customer, speaker and role, product and version, proof unit, audience, channel, market and locale, evidence cutoff, usage horizon, and external-action boundary.

## 2. Executive Finding

State what current proof can and cannot support. Name the primary source, metric, quote, causality, permission, privacy, freshness, format, or approval constraint. Do not return an unexplained proof score or publish-ready claim.

## 3. Source, Proof, And Permission Ledger

Include source IDs, customer and role, relationship, product, workflow, period, exact quote or metric, calculation, evidence state, conditions, counterevidence, confidentiality, incentive, usage rights, approval, expiry, and unavailable inputs.

## 4. Claim And Risk Review

Show proposed wording, source proof, scope, qualifier, prevalence boundary, causal boundary, regulated or high-risk issue, customer approval, internal owner, prohibited expansion, and smallest evidence needed.

## 5. Atomic Proof Units

Return only source-traceable exact quotes, bounded paraphrases, approved translations, reproducible metrics, workflow proof, outcome proof, reference briefs, or proof cards. State context, limitations, current status, usage rights, and expiry beside each.

## 6. Story And Derivative Drafts

Show situation, prior alternative, decision, implementation, first and repeated value, outcomes, tradeoffs, mixed results, current state, best-fit and poor-fit conditions, source IDs, and approval status. For every derivative show edits, format, channel, audience, market, language, disclosure, accessibility, lineage, version, and prohibited uses.

## 7. Privacy, Approval, And Library Controls

Show identity treatment, reidentification risk, data minimization, incentive disclosure, customer and internal reviewers, permissions, effective and expiry dates, usage locations, refresh, correction, withdrawal, archive, removal, and retirement.

## 8. Next Evidence And Handoffs

Return prioritized evidence and permission requests with owner, source, decision consequence, review date, completion proof, specialist handoff, and pinned Playbook source. Keep customer contact, approval, publishing, and system changes unexecuted.

## Mode-Specific Requirements

| Mode | Required addition |
| --- | --- |
| `audit` | active proof inventory, lineage, claim and metric defects, permission gaps, usage locations, stale and withdrawn risks, and remediation |
| `develop` | evidence-led narrative or atomic proof draft, unsupported sections omitted, review package, and derivative boundaries |
| `library` | taxonomy, IDs, relationships, states, approvals, access, usage map, refresh, withdrawal, archive, and retirement workflow |

## Final Checks

- Customer, speaker, role, product, version, source, period, quote, metric, outcome, and permission remain compatible.
- Direct quote, paraphrase, translation, calculation, customer report, association, and causal claim remain distinct.
- Mixed outcomes and material conditions are not hidden.
- Permission is item-, channel-, market-, language-, duration-, and purpose-specific.
- No identity, quote, metric, result, testimonial, customer relationship, regulated claim, permission, approval, or citation is invented.
- No system access, customer contact, signature, publication, claim change, deployment, or result claim appears.
