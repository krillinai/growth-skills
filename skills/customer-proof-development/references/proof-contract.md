# Proof Contract

Complete this contract before auditing, drafting, adapting, approving, publishing, or reusing customer proof.

## Decision Header

| Field | Requirement |
| --- | --- |
| Mode | `audit`, `develop`, or `library` |
| Decision | The buyer, customer, product, content, sales, press, or governance decision the proof supports |
| Owner | Accountable internal role, customer approver, reviewers, and review date |
| Customer | Legal entity, brand, relationship, speaker, role, account, market, and identity state |
| Product | Product, edition, plan, version, implementation, workflow, and availability period |
| Proof unit | Exact quote, metric, outcome, artifact, logo, screenshot, or bounded claim |
| Usage | Audience, format, channel, market, language, duration, and derivative rights |
| Action | Local draft, approval-ready specification, or separately authorized publication |

## Evidence States

Use only `verified`, `inferred`, `unavailable`, or `not applicable` for evidence-bearing rows. Keep a customer, seller, partner, employee, stakeholder, or vendor statement without inspectable support as a `reported signal` with no evidence state.

Record source ID, artifact, owner, access, customer, speaker, role, product and version, market, date, period, method, exact support, evidence state, limitations, counterevidence, confidentiality, consent, and requested action for every material claim.

## Customer And Relationship Record

Record legal customer, public brand, business unit, account, user, buyer, payer, beneficiary, champion, administrator, executive, implementation partner, and quoted speaker separately. State whether the relationship is prospect, trial, active, former, partner, reference, paid participant, or unavailable, with evidence and effective date.

A public logo or past contract does not verify current use, endorsement, product version, result, or reference availability.

## Proof Unit Record

Every atomic unit includes:

- source and immutable source excerpt or calculation;
- customer, speaker, role, product, workflow, market, and period;
- exact quote, bounded paraphrase, translation, metric, visual, or outcome;
- evidence state, context, qualifiers, counterevidence, uncertainty, and current status;
- approved public wording and prohibited expansions;
- consent, usage rights, incentive and disclosure;
- derivative IDs, locations, version, owner, review, expiry, withdrawal, and retirement.

Do not merge several customers into one unit. Do not replace unavailable fields with plausible details.

## Story Boundary

Record prior state and alternative, problem and trigger, selection criteria, implementation and support, first and repeated value, outcome, metric, costs, timing, mixed or negative results, current state, best-fit conditions, poor-fit conditions, and limitations. Every section must point to proof units or be labeled an internal narrative choice with no expanded factual claim.

## Permission Matrix

Separate identity, name, logo, title, quote, paraphrase, recording, transcript, screenshot, product data, metric, pricing, contract, case, reference, sales, website, ad, social, press, analyst, event, translation, localization, sublicensing, archive, and model-training rights. Record exact item, wording, edits, channels, audiences, markets, languages, effective date, duration, expiry, withdrawal, correction, deletion, and approvers.

## Privacy Boundary

Use the minimum authorized evidence. Prefer customer-approved excerpts, aggregates, redaction, or pseudonymization. Record purpose, authority, consent, access, retention, correction, deletion, security, minimum cells, suppression, and reidentification risk. Exclude credentials, tax identifiers, exact location, protected traits, health or financial details, private messages, security findings, employee notes, and full contracts unless separately necessary, authorized, and governed.
