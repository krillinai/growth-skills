# Governance, Experiments, And Output

## Noncompensable Protections

Do not average away identity, consent, accessibility, fraud, moderation, payment security, insurance, worker or provider safety, customer protection, dispute handling, incident response, or appeals.

For each protection, record affected participants and nonparticipants, inherent exposure, control design, owner, implementation, operation, exceptions, incidents, recovery, tolerance, residual exposure, acceptance authority, verification, stop condition, expiry, and residual obligations.

Use `blocked`, `conditional`, or `not assessable` when a hard protection fails. Strong growth or economic evidence cannot compensate for harm.

## Marketplace Experiments

Marketplaces create cross-side interference, migration, substitution, congestion, price and availability equilibrium, strategic response, carryover, and network spillovers. A user-level independent A/B test may be invalid.

Define:

- intervention, decision, estimand, assignment unit, exposure unit, atomic market, sides, horizon, and maturity;
- direct, cross-side, spillover, equilibrium, displacement, and total effects;
- availability, discovery, response, match, fulfillment, quality, participant economics, repeat, retention, and guardrails;
- contamination, migration, anticipation, novelty, carryover, reversals, and post-treatment persistence.

Consider cluster, geographic, switchback, saturation, phased, threshold, matched, synthetic-control, or other mechanism-compatible designs. Route power, assignment, SRM, diagnostics, inference, and readout to `experiment-design`. Separate observed result, attribution, causal estimate, uncertainty, and decision.

## AI And Automation

For ranking, matching, pricing, eligibility, fraud, support, or allocation automation, record input, source, purpose, prompt or policy, model, tool, version, output, reviewer, authority, action, and outcome.

Require minimum-necessary data, lineage, reproducibility, validation, calibration, drift, reason codes, access, audit, abstention, human review, correction, appeal, override, stop, rollback, incident, and recovery. Check feedback loops, concentration, cold-start disadvantage, leakage, hallucination, unsafe action, cost, latency, reliability, and participant harm.

Keep draft, recommendation, approval, authorization, execution, verification, and result separate. Do not use protected traits, private messages, identity documents, payment credentials, or sensitive employee data to rank participants or automate consequential decisions.

## Market Transfer

For China or any new market, revalidate market, language, locale, currency, timezone, legal entity, participant roles, geography, category, service model, platform, app distribution, maps, identity, payment, invoice, messaging, reviews, moderation, data, privacy, support, vendor, staffing, labor, tax, and decision rights.

Require current direct evidence and locally accountable expertise. Translation, currency conversion, a US operating model, or another city's thresholds do not establish local availability, compliance, economics, trust, or launch readiness.

## Output Contract

Return these sections in order:

1. `assessment header`: mode, decision, owner, customer, participants, payer, atomic market, interaction, horizon, scope, hard constraints, and boundary;
2. `local verdict`: working, constrained, fragile, congested or harmful, not assessable, or not applicable, with evidence coverage and limiting dependency;
3. `state maps`: side-specific eligible, active, unavailable, failed, fulfilled, repeated, and exited states joined at the core interaction;
4. `market ledger`: supply, demand, discovery, response, matching, fulfillment, quality, time, repeat, retention, concentration, and maturity;
5. `economic ledger`: participant and platform value, revenue, full variable cost, contribution, incentives, utilization, support, and uncertainty;
6. `trust and governance`: protections, incidents, controls, residual exposure, acceptance, appeals, and stop conditions;
7. `decision sequence`: smallest next disposition, prerequisite, owner, evidence gate, capacity gate, graduation, pause, stop, rollback, and verification;
8. `handoffs and completion`: specialist boundaries, approval-ready requests, Playbook sources, local artifacts, external actions not taken, expiry, and residual obligations.

Do not add one marketplace score, universal liquidity threshold, invented target, date, owner, budget, forecast, or impact claim.

## External Actions

This Skill may create local specifications, metric contracts, state maps, evidence manifests, decision briefs, and approval-ready handoffs. It must not access authenticated systems, export private or production data, recruit or ban participants, issue rewards, spend, change eligibility, ranking, pricing, payments, reviews, permissions, or policy, message participants, launch markets or experiments, publish findings, or deploy systems without separate task-level authorization and specialist review.
