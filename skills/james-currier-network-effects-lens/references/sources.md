## Attribution Rules

- Prefer complete talks and direct NFX material over clips and third-party summaries.
- Treat taxonomies as diagnostic maps, not guaranteed business outcomes.
- Separate Currier's claims from interviewer framing and company examples.
- Treat AI-era statements as developing hypotheses that require current product evidence.
- Do not copy long passages. Summarize the method and link the source.
- Do not imply endorsement, affiliation, private access, or current company facts.
- When first-party product evidence conflicts with the framework, preserve the evidence and lower confidence in the framework-based inference.
