## Source Rules

- Cite exact pinned sections and preserve the Playbook commit.
- Keep Playbook concepts separate from direct event, customer, identity, market, source, outcome, and causal evidence.
- Record entity, eligibility, value event, opportunity, product, market, identity, source, window, cutoff, timezone, maturity, definition version, evidence state, limitation, and transfer boundary.
- Do not turn a framework, case, expert statement, benchmark, curve, ratio, dashboard, or public page into a complete distribution, private fact, universal target, cause, implementation, or result.
- Use current direct sources and accountable specialists for volatile provider, platform, market, benchmark, rights, legal, regulatory, privacy, and security claims.
- Record a newer Playbook commit explicitly rather than silently mixing versions.
