# Product Launch Strategy Output Contract

## Required Order

Return:

1. mode, decision, owner, release unit and version, audience, market, horizon, and action boundary;
2. release, customer, value, audience, access, evidence, limitation, and dependency contract;
3. readiness decision and first blocking dependency;
4. audience and access ladder;
5. product, experience, measurement, operations, distribution, risk, and rollback plan;
6. state and cohort reconciliation from attention to mature outcome;
7. decision, portfolio, and post-launch learning records;
8. specialist handoffs, pinned sources, and unresolved evidence.

## Release And Readiness Ledger

| Release unit and version | Customer and value | Initial audience | Access and exposure | Product gate | Experience gate | Measurement gate | Operations gate | Distribution gate | Decision and evidence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Use `unavailable` rather than inventing private readiness. Keep product, feature, migration, market, commercial, and communication-only units separate.

## Audience Ladder

| Stage | Learning decision | Eligibility and exclusions | Access and capacity | First and repeated value | Guardrails and maturity | Advance, hold, repair, pause, rollback | Owner and proof |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Do not present the ladder as authorization to grant access or communicate.

## State Reconciliation

| Cohort and version | Attention or demand | Eligible and assigned | Enabled and exposed | Entered | First value | Repeated value | Mature customer and business outcome | Residuals and limits |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | --- | --- |

Use compatible entities, definitions, windows, and maturity. Explain residuals from duplicates, ineligibility, no-response, access expiry, identity loss, unsupported states, errors, rollback, late events, and censoring.

## Operations And Handoffs

| Dependency | Owner and receiver | Input and output | Capacity and service expectation | Approval and exception | Guardrail and incident | Completion proof |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Recommendations are local specifications, not product, access, campaign, publishing, budget, or launch actions.

## Privacy And External Action

Use decision-required aggregate, redacted, or pseudonymous evidence. Exclude credentials, direct contacts, private messages, payment credentials, tax IDs, protected attributes, exact locations, raw support content, employee notes, full graphs, and unrelated histories.

Local strategy does not authorize system access, customer exports, feature flags, product changes, access grants, migrations, pricing, packages, contracts, tracking, pages, budgets, outreach, publication, advertising, spending, experiments, deployment, or launch. Record accountable operational owners, approvals, privacy, security, legal or policy, finance, support, incident, validation, monitoring, fallback, rollback, and audit controls as handoffs.
