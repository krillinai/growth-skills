## Attribution Rules

- Treat the 40% result as an empirical heuristic shaped by survey design and segment.
- Do not attribute Dropbox, LogMeIn, Eventbrite, or Lookout outcomes to one person.
- Distinguish first-person accounts from independently verified causal evidence.
- Do not imply endorsement, private access, or current company facts.
- Preserve first-party product evidence when it conflicts with the framework.
