## Attribution Rules

- Prefer Dunford's books, own channel, and full talks over short clips and summaries.
- Treat client examples as partial accounts, not controlled causal studies.
- Do not claim positioning creates PMF, retention, or product quality.
- Do not imply endorsement, affiliation, private access, or current client facts.
- When customer and product evidence conflicts with a framework assumption, preserve the evidence and revise the position.
