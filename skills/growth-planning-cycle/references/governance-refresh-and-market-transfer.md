# Governance, Refresh, And Market Transfer

## Separate Planning And Authority States

```text
evidence -> analysis -> recommendation -> owner decision -> required approval -> bounded authorization -> execution -> verification -> mature result
```

Record the owner, approver, conditions, effective period, expiry, and attributable proof at each applicable state. Executive comments are not decisions. A proposed budget is not approval. Naming an owner is not acceptance. A presentation is not authorization, implementation, or completion.

## Establish One Plan Of Record

The plan of record contains:

- cycle contract and current state;
- artifact manifest and compatibility results;
- decisions, alternatives, rationale, conditions, exclusions, and unresolved items;
- portfolio, capacity, budget, dependency, maturity, and risk reconciliations;
- downstream initiative versions, owner acceptance, approvals, and permission boundaries;
- review calendar, refresh triggers, version history, and residual obligations.

Keep evidence and specialist artifacts in their owning records and link by stable ID and version. One canonical record reduces coordination; it must not flatten incompatible details or replace customer contact and decisions.

## Govern Refreshes

Refresh when a predeclared trigger or attributable material change affects strategy, metric, baseline, target, forecast, opportunity, portfolio, capacity, budget, market, platform, risk, approval, initiative, or maturity.

For every refresh record:

| Field | Requirement |
| --- | --- |
| Trigger | Event, new evidence, miss, contradiction, maturity, expiry, incident, or decision change |
| Prior state | Cycle and artifact versions, cutoff, assumptions, decisions, approvals, commitments, and results available at the time |
| New state | New cutoff, supplied evidence, changed specialist versions, and unresolved inputs |
| Bridge | Data, definition, method, assumption, decision, scope, resource, and market changes |
| Effect | Portfolio, capacity, budget, initiative, risk, customer, timing, and review consequences |
| Decision | Preserve, revise, defer, pause, stop, cancel, supersede, or escalate |
| Governance | Owner, approver, effective date, expiry, communication boundary, and next review |

Do not backdate assumptions, overwrite forecasts with actuals, move targets to hide misses, relabel failed initiatives, or erase prior decisions. A new plan can be better without pretending it was always intended.

## Hand Off To Operating Review

Provide the operating review with the current cycle version, source decisions, target and forecast versions, portfolio and initiative states, mature metrics, capacity and budget boundaries, open gates, risks, commitments, changes, and requested decisions. The review records evidence and decisions; it does not silently change the plan of record.

## Transfer Across Markets

Treat each market as a separate evidence and operating boundary. Revalidate customer and payer roles, product, value, channels, platforms, metrics, baseline, targets, forecasts, opportunities, prices, currency, payments, invoices, costs, capacity, budgets, data, consent, advertising, calendars, holidays, support, risk, owners, approval, and rollback.

For mainland China, separate market from Simplified Chinese language and distinguish legal entity, locale, app distribution, identity, payment, invoice, data, consent, content, advertising, holidays, staffing, lead times, and locally accountable review. Translation and currency conversion do not establish transfer. Use current direct sources and local expertise; avoid legal, tax, regulatory, provider-availability, accounting, approval, or performance conclusions.

## Protect People And Data

Use aggregate, redacted, pseudonymous, minimum-necessary evidence. Define purpose, authority, consent where required, access, retention, deletion, audit, and small-cell suppression. Use accountable roles rather than unnecessary names.

Do not expose credentials, payment details, private messages, health or financial data, protected traits, precise location, compensation, performance ratings, employee reviews, raw identity, or full histories. Do not rank individuals for consequential targeting, pricing, service, employment, credit, insurance, health, housing, or similar decisions. Escalate sensitive use to accountable review without claiming approval.

## Prepare External Actions Without Executing

For every requested action record owner, system, exact mutation or message, affected population, data, budget, duration, prerequisites, approvers, least privilege, dry run, monitoring, cap, stop, rollback, correction, audit, and completion proof.

Without separate task-level authorization, create local artifacts only. Do not access systems, export data, assign owners, approve, change targets or budgets, hire, reorganize, launch, contact, publish, send, spend, procure, deploy, or claim results.

## Preserve Residual Obligations

Supersession, cancellation, expiry, or completion does not erase customer support, refunds, communication, data deletion, access removal, monitoring, rollback, vendor, contractual, security, maintenance, documentation, or decommissioning obligations. Assign owner, date, evidence, escalation, and verification.
