# Growth Planning Cycle Output Contract

## Required Order

Return:

1. mode, cycle ID and version, state, cadence, horizon, scope, owners, forums, approvers, cutoff, effective period, review, refresh, expiry, and external-action boundary;
2. artifact manifest with source, version, owner, as-of, evidence, availability, approval, lineage, contradiction, and missing-input states;
3. compatibility matrix and reconciliation bridges across entities, markets, periods, maturity, currencies, accounting, metrics, and versions;
4. strategy, baseline, target, forecast, opportunity, scenario, investment, portfolio, organization, initiative, and review handoffs;
5. ordered decision graph with alternatives, exclusions, dependencies, owners, approvers, earliest valid and latest useful dates, expiry, and escalation;
6. portfolio outcome, overlap, capacity, budget boundary, maintenance, opportunity cost, scenario, and risk reconciliation;
7. decision calendar, evidence and approval gates, initiative readiness, operating reviews, and refresh triggers;
8. plan of record, version history, unresolved decisions, residual obligations, pinned sources, approval-ready external actions, and completion record.

## Artifact Manifest

| Artifact | ID / version / owner | Decision and scope | Source / as-of / cutoff / maturity | Evidence and availability | Approval / effective / expiry | Lineage / contradiction / refresh |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Use `verified`, `inferred`, `unavailable`, or `not applicable` for evidence and `required`, `conditional`, `optional`, `unavailable`, `superseded`, or `not applicable` for planning availability.

## Compatibility And Reconciliation Matrix

| Quantity or artifact | Entity / eligibility / identity | Customer / product / market / segment | Definition / window / cohort / maturity | Period / cutoff / version | Currency / accounting / source | Compatible view, bridge, or unresolved conflict |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Do not blend incompatible units or select favorable versions.

Keep recommendation, decision, approval, authorization, action, verification, and result separate.

Preserve old assumptions, misses, forecasts, targets, commitments, decisions, and evidence cutoffs.

## Completion Gate

Confirm that cycle, version, mode, state, cadence, horizon, fiscal and calendar basis, owner, forum, approver, scope, exclusions, customer, participant, product, model, market, locale, strategy, metrics, baseline, target, opportunity, forecast, scenario, investment, organization, portfolio, initiative, risk, source, evidence, cutoff, maturity, compatibility, lineage, contradiction, hierarchy, overlap, residual, decisions, alternatives, dependencies, capacity, budget, maintenance, opportunity cost, calendar, gates, approval, effective period, refresh, expiry, review, privacy, market transfer, residual obligations, handoffs, pinned sources, and external actions are explicit; downstream tasks did not become strategy; targets did not become forecasts or evidence; favorable versions were not cherry-picked; incompatible units were not blended; outcomes and resources were not duplicated; all work did not become top priority; ceremonies did not override maturity; customer protection was not removed; upside did not erase risk; history was not rewritten; approvals, execution, completion, and results were not invented; and no external action occurred.
