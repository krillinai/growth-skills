# Growth Anomaly Investigation Output Contract

## Required Order

Return:

1. mode, incident ID, reported symptom, decision, owner, deadline, scope, status, evidence cutoff, and external-action boundary;
2. frozen metric, entity, population, source, identity, product, segment, market, window, timezone, baseline, expected variation, and maturity contract;
3. source reconciliation and data-quality register;
4. prior and current counts, denominators, rates or values, absolute and relative deltas, uncertainty, missingness, and maturity;
5. contribution-preserving decomposition with mix, within-segment, interaction or residual, coverage, and reconciliation;
6. change timeline and candidate-mechanism ledger with support, contradiction, alternatives, causal status, and discriminating evidence;
7. `measurement issue`, `behavioral change`, `mixed`, `expected variation`, or `unresolved` resolution with confidence boundary and impact;
8. next evidence, containment or monitoring recommendation, decision rules, owners, maturity, stop, and closure criteria;
9. privacy, market transfer, governance, specialist handoffs, pinned Growth Playbook sources, approval-ready external actions, and completion record.

## Metric And Source Contract

| Field | Definition | Source and version | Owner | Quality, maturity, limitation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Include entity, event or state, numerator, denominator, eligibility, identity, unit, aggregation, exclusions, event time, processing time, timezone, windows, cutoff, lateness, corrections, and source lineage.

## Observed Change Register

| View | Prior | Current | Absolute delta | Relative delta | Count / denominator | Baseline and uncertainty | Maturity | Limitation |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Keep rate and count, revenue and contribution, attributed and incremental, observed and forecast, zero and missing, and provisional and mature states distinct.

## Source Reconciliation Register

| Source | Contract and coverage | Version and cutoff | Reported value | Compatible value | Difference | Explanation | Residual and next check |
| --- | --- | --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | --- | --- |

Do not average incompatible sources or force residuals to zero.

| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

State the decomposition convention. Keep descriptive contribution distinct from causal effect.

## Resolution Record

| Resolution state | Material evidence | Measurement contribution | Behavioral contribution | Expected variation | Unknown residual | Confidence boundary | Decision implication |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Use one primary state only when evidence distinguishes it. Do not invent a confidence score.

## Evidence And Action Register

| Evidence or proposed action | Question or decision | Required artifact or mutation | Owner | Maturity or date | Positive / negative / inconclusive rule | Permission | Stop / rollback / closure |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Separate read-only evidence tasks from external actions. Do not imply execution.

## Completion Gate

Confirm incident, decision, owner, metric, entity, population, numerator, denominator, eligibility, identity, source, version, product, segment, market, timezone, baseline, comparison, expected variation, event and processing time, cutoff, lateness, maturity, counts, rates, absolute and relative deltas, uncertainty, missingness, reconciliation, mix, within-segment contribution, residual, timeline, candidates, contradictions, alternatives, causal status, resolution, confidence boundary, impact, evidence plan, monitoring, containment, closure, privacy, market transfer, handoffs, pinned sources, and external actions are explicit; partial periods did not become mature outcomes; missing did not become zero; rates did not hide counts; incompatible dashboards were not averaged; aggregate changes did not hide mix reversals; segment search did not become proof; benchmark gaps did not become anomalies; timing and attribution did not become causality; unsupported cause, precision, confidence, approval, execution, resolution, and business impact were not invented; and no external action occurred.
