# TikTok Ads Audit Methods

Analyze compatible supplied evidence only. Use the account's own history and declared economics, not universal creative or media thresholds.

## Dependency Order

```text
international TikTok scope and decision
-> event and input integrity
-> structure and paid delivery
-> creative concepts and variants
-> Spark identity, rights, and organic-versus-paid evidence
-> destination and business reconciliation
-> bounded findings and decision plan
```

Continue with supported observations when evidence is partial, but name blocked decisions.

## Structure And Delivery

Use stable IDs to inventory campaign, ad group, ad, creative, identity, placement, optimization goal, destination, schedule, status, and result definition. Describe spend, impressions, clicks, views, conversions, and value distributions only at compatible grains.

Possible descriptive measures include spend share, CPM, click-through rate, cost per click, declared video-view rates, platform conversion rate, platform cost per result, and attributed value per cost. Show denominator, date, market, format, currency, result type, and attribution setting. Zero denominators produce `unavailable`.

Concentration is not automatically a defect. Report who or what receives delivery, how it changes, and the downstream customer or operating risk. Do not prescribe a universal campaign count, audience breadth, placement, budget split, or optimization strategy.

## Creative Concepts And Variants

Map persona, situation, problem, promise, proof, offer, CTA, creator, identity, hook, narrative, sound, caption, language, locale, format, duration, destination, and rights. Treat a changed customer belief or value hypothesis as a possible new concept; treat alternate openings, edits, crops, lengths, captions, subtitles, sounds, and aspect ratios as variants when the hypothesis is unchanged.

View and engagement measures are attention signals under their exported definitions, not received customer value. Do not impose a universal first-seconds hook rate, completion rate, duration, native style, creator count, or creative volume. Compare compatible formats, delivery, audiences, markets, dates, and outcomes.

### Fatigue Signals

Require a comparable time series and delivery context. Examine sustained changes in attention, qualified outcomes, cost, repeated reach, concept diversity, creator mix, placement, audience, destination, offer, seasonality, auction conditions, and measurement. Use `fatigue signal` unless evidence distinguishes creative decay from other causes. Never use one universal frequency threshold.

## Spark Ads

Separate:

1. identity and public post reference;
2. supplied authorization status, scope, and expiry;
3. organic post metrics and context;
4. paid delivery and attributed results;
5. aggregated business outcomes and incrementality.

A public post, creator label, or existing engagement does not establish ownership, permission, endorsement, authenticity, or usage rights. Organic engagement is selected historical evidence, not a valid control. Never request, store, redeem, renew, or expose a Spark authorization code.

## Destination Continuity

Compare creative audience state, promise, product, price, promotion, proof, CTA, language, locale, device path, and next value with the dated web, app, lead, or shop state. Mark app, deep-link, shop, form, speed, checkout, consent, and tracking issues inferred until measured. Never edit or deploy a destination.

## Measurement And Reconciliation

Build a ledger for event name, source, optimization status, counting, value, attribution setting, timezone, currency, Pixel, Events API, app or MMP path, identity, deduplication, consent, new-customer logic, refunds, maturation, and business definition.

Keep these layers separate:

1. paid delivery reported by TikTok;
2. platform-attributed web, app, lead, or shop outcomes;
3. organic post or creator evidence;
4. business-system qualified customer, revenue, refund, contribution, and retained value;
5. incremental outcomes supported by a credible counterfactual.

Do not force layers to match. Reconcile dates, entities, windows, click, view, or engaged-view credit, modeled results, app privacy frameworks, MMP settings, deduplication, consent, currency, cancellations, refunds, creator costs, late outcomes, and unavailable records. Attribution and organic lift are not incrementality.

## Customer Quality And Economics

When definitions align, calculate qualified customer acquisition cost, refund-adjusted revenue, contribution, contribution after media and declared creator cost, payback, and matured retained value. Expose included costs. Do not infer new-customer status, margin, capacity, retention, or payback from platform purchases, leads, or value per cost.

## Reported Change Diagnosis

Translate "TikTok performance changed" into metric, definition, entity, market, format, placement, destination, comparison window, attribution setting, magnitude, and source. Mark it `reported signal` until verified. Check:

1. exports, events, attribution, Pixel, Events API, app or MMP, consent, and definitions;
2. maturity, dates, seasonality, policy, product, price, inventory, and creator events;
3. spend, auction, placement, audience, optimization, and delivery mix;
4. concepts, variants, creators, identities, Spark status, and destinations;
5. qualified customer, refund, contribution, capacity, and retained value;
6. causal evidence.

Return competing explanations and the smallest discriminating evidence. Do not jump from a correlated movement to a creative, audience, bid, budget, or campaign action.

## Findings

For every finding include observation, decision impact, source and date, evidence state, bounded action, completion proof, owner, and relative effort. Sequence the 30-day plan by dependency, with guardrails and review dates. Use declared economics or prospectively agreed experiment rules; never fabricate thresholds.
