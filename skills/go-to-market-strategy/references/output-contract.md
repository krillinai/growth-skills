Return Markdown unless the user requests another local format. Keep public signals, supplied evidence, observations, attribution, causal estimates, forecasts, scenarios, targets, plans, benchmarks, approvals, and operating actions separate.

## 1. Decision And GTM Header

State mode, decision, owner, product and offer version, customer and buying unit, initial segment, market, motion, horizon, evidence cutoff, review date, non-goals, and external-action boundary.

| Motion or route | Customer and intent | Value and buying path | Offer and destination | Handoffs and capacity | Economics and maturity | Evidence, risk, stop |

## 5. End-To-End State Map

Show reachable eligible customer, qualified demand, exposure, response or buying milestones, product access, first value, repeated value, purchase, renewal, contribution, cash, advocacy or expansion, and next decision with entities, owners, counts, rates, cohorts, maturity, and handoffs.

## 6. Pilot And Sequence

Return prerequisites, beachhead, non-goals, cohort and caps, product and channel exposure, delivery, measurement, capacity, positive, negative, harmful, and inconclusive outcomes, decision rule, stop, rollback, expiry, and adjacent-unit gate.

## 7. Measurement, Economics, And Portfolio

Show actual, forecast, scenario, target, and plan separately; source and attribution status; retained value, contribution, cash, CAC, LTV, payback, uncertainty, capacity, concentration, mix, segment reversals, and portfolio interactions where compatible.

## 8. Governance And Completion

List owners, cadence, privacy and fairness, market-transfer gaps, specialist handoffs, pinned Playbook sources, approval-ready actions, refresh and retirement. Confirm no hidden blocker, fabricated evidence, opaque score, causal overclaim, unauthorized access, commercial or product change, contact, campaign, launch, publication, spend, deployment, or result claim.
