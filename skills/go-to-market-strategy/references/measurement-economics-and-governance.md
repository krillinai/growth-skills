# Measurement, Economics, And Governance

GTM measurement must preserve the customer and business value chain without turning attribution, forecasts, targets, or attention into outcomes.

## State And Metric Contract

Define entity, eligibility, identity, source, product and offer version, market, route, assignment or access, exposure, state, event, numerator, denominator, window, cohort, maturity, cutoff, currency, value and cost basis, exclusions, quality, owner, and decision.

Keep separate:

```text
attention and public signal
-> reachable eligible customer
-> qualified demand or intent
-> message and offer exposure
-> response or buying milestone
-> product access and first value
-> repeated value and retention
-> purchase, renewal, contribution, and cash
-> advocacy, expansion, or next GTM decision
```

Report counts beside rates and absolute losses. Missing and immature are not zero. Same-period leads and revenue are not one cohort when buying and value cycles cross periods.

## Attribution, Causality, And Forecasts

Attribution assigns operational credit. Incrementality estimates outcomes caused relative to a counterfactual. Forecasts predict future outcomes under an information set. Targets state desired outcomes. Plans state intended actions. Preserve each class and its version.

Do not call a message, price, route, partner, seller, product, or launch causal from before-after movement. Define assignment or alternative evidence, exposure, interference, overrides, sample, maturity, uncertainty, guardrails, and decision rules. Route causal design and interpretation to `experiment-design`.

## Retained Economics

Connect compatible acquisition cohorts to activation, retained value, conversion, realized price, renewal, expansion, contraction, churn, refunds, delivery cost, gross profit, contribution, cash, CAC, LTV, and payback. Keep attributed, incremental, average, and marginal economics separate.

Do not backsolve a model to hit revenue, growth, LTV:CAC, payback, or margin targets. Route detailed economics to `unit-economics-analysis`, LTV to `ltv-analysis`, forecasts to `growth-forecasting`, pricing to `pricing-and-packaging-strategy`, and recurring-value movement to `growth-accounting`.

## Decision And Operating Cadence

For every review state decision, owner, source cutoff, GTM unit and version, readiness gates, evidence and counterevidence, forecast and target gap, capacity, customer and business guardrails, threshold, next evidence, approval boundary, actualization, correction, rollback, expiry, refresh, and retirement.

Use weekly or natural-cycle operational review and monthly or stage-gate strategic review only when appropriate to the product and buying cycle. Track stale evidence, definition drift, source quality, handoff failures, capacity, concentration, forecast error, segment reversals, customer harm, and decisions reversed.

## Privacy And Fairness

Use minimum necessary, aggregate, permissioned, redacted, pseudonymous, and small-cell-suppressed inputs. Define purpose, authority, consent, access, lineage, retention, deletion, correction, fairness review, audit, incident, fallback, and retirement.

Do not request payment credentials, protected traits, health or financial details, private messages, exact locations, employee notes, or complete device graphs when aggregates suffice. Do not scrape, rank people by sensitive value, personalize prices, suppress service, or contact anyone without separate authority and controls. Segment strategy is not permission for individual treatment.

## Market Transfer And China

Revalidate customer and payer, job, alternatives, position, proof, product, offer, price, motion, route, channel, platform, app distribution, payment, invoice, tax, contract, identity, consent, data, storage, transfer, support, advertising, measurement, attribution, economics, source coverage, and owner by market.

Translation and currency conversion do not validate a local GTM. For mainland China, use current direct sources and locally accountable review for time-sensitive platform, payment, app, data, advertising, and provider conditions. Do not provide legal, tax, accounting, regulatory, or provider conclusions.

## External Actions

Local artifacts may specify contracts, readiness, paths, pilots, measurement, and handoffs. Account access, data exports, customer or prospect contact, campaigns, publishing, product or commercial changes, budget changes, experiments, deployments, filings, procurement, and result claims require separate task-level authorization, accountable owners, controls, monitoring, and rollback.
