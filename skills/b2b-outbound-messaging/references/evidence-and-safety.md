# Evidence And Safety

## Canonical Evidence States

Use exactly one evidence state for every evidence-bearing row:

| State | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| `verified` | An inspected captured source directly supports the exact statement within its recorded scope. |
| `inferred` | A bounded conclusion follows from named verified inputs but is not itself directly stated; record basis and confirmation step. |
| `unavailable` | Needed evidence is absent, inaccessible, declined, stale for the claim, or outside the captured scope. |
| `not applicable` | The evidence question does not apply to this decision. |

`reported signal` is a signal status, never a fifth evidence state. Use it only for an attributable stakeholder statement that has not been independently inspected, and leave evidence state blank. Ordinary working hypotheses are `inferred`, explicitly labeled, and never asserted as recipient facts.

Public evidence is enough to start bounded work. Attributable private artifacts are optional and may be used only within their supplied purpose, recipient, field, and retention scope. Treat declined or missing CRM, contact, intent, enrichment, analytics, or conversation data as `unavailable`, not failure. Do not repeatedly request declined access.

Never invent a recipient event, interest, relationship, referral, engagement, pain, project, or personal belief. Never invent metrics, customer names, testimonials, prices, availability, guarantees, awards, certifications, regulatory status, capabilities, results, or legal conclusions.

## Permission Is A Context Record

Channel availability, a public profile, a public company address, a work title, a mutual connection, silence, or a language choice never establishes permission. Record:

- relationship and exact contact source;
- intended recipient and bounded purpose;
- market, language, locale, and channel separately;
- real sender and organization;
- consent or other owner-approved permission basis as supplied;
- applicable owner/legal review and its status;
- current platform and account policy source/status;
- opt-out, do-not-contact, complaint, bounce, withdrawal, and suppression state;
- sequence limit, cadence owner, and material changes since approval.

If the actual permission basis, account-policy status, or stop state is unresolved, do not draft message copy. Pending legal or platform review may allow a local review draft only when those three fields are supplied; it blocks final/sendable status and all external action. It must carry both exact literals `local review only` and `not final or sendable`; both labels are required and not paraphrasable. Ask the fewest questions needed and retain useful public or supplied evidence. Do not claim compliance. This Skill is not legal advice; the owner remains responsible for current market, sector, recipient, platform, and account review.

## Prohibited Data And Conduct

Never:

- scrape or bulk-collect contacts, profiles, messages, or platform data;
- discover personal emails or mobile numbers, enrich personal profiles, or infer sensitive/personal traits;
- expose raw contact details, home addresses, health, ethnicity, religion, politics, finances, family details, or other unnecessary personal data;
- leak private URLs, credentials, tokens, internal record links, or supplied data beyond its attributable scope;
- impersonate, spoof, use a false sender, access another person's account, or use lookalike domains;
- fabricate a prior relationship or use deceptive `Re:`/`Fwd:` framing;
- hide opt-out, obscure sender identity, use misleading urgency, or imply false scarcity;
- bypass authentication, access controls, CAPTCHA, rate limits, platform limits, or account policy.

Remove prohibited data from drafts and ledgers rather than repeating it in a refusal. Preserve only the minimum description needed to explain why it was excluded.

## External-Action Boundary

Drafting authorizes local text only. It never authorizes sending, connection requests, attachments, contact import, account access, CRM reads or writes, automation setup, list upload, bulk collection, campaign launch, or monitoring. Contact discovery and enrichment remain out of scope even when requested.

For a legitimate next step, provide a separate handoff that states the exact proposed action, system/account, recipient scope, sender, purpose, permission owner, stop/suppression checks, current platform capability source, reviewer, rollback or correction path, and task-level authorization still required. Do not perform the action as part of this Skill.

## Completion Check

Confirm that every personalization claim has a ledger row; canonical states are exact; `reported signal` rows have blank evidence state; hypotheses remain questions or possibilities; unsupported claims are excluded; private data is minimized; sender identity is real; the permission record is complete; every pending-review draft contains exact label `local review only`; every pending-review draft contains exact label `not final or sendable`; neither label was paraphrased; all current stop states were applied; cadence is owner-approved; no benchmark or platform capability was invented; market, language, locale, and channel remain separate; and no external action occurred.
