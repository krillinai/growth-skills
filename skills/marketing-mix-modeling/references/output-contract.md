# Marketing Mix Modeling Output Contract

## Required Order

Return:

1. mode, model ID and version, state, predictive, structural, and decision verdicts, decision, outcome, aggregate unit, markets, time, owners, cutoff, review, expiry, privacy, and external actions;
2. outcome, source, evidence, data-vintage, currency, accounting, maturity, reconciliation, missingness, revision, and limitation manifest;
3. media, owned, earned, product, price, promotion, inventory, distribution, competitor, calendar, macro, and contextual-input panel;
4. feasibility, variation, history, dimensionality, lag, sparsity, structural-break, collinearity, endogeneity, confounding, interference, and identification audit;
5. transformation, prior, constraint, hierarchy, interaction, estimation, software, and code specification;
6. predictive, residual, posterior or simulation, structural, sensitivity, contribution, experiment-calibration, and decision validation;
7. baseline, media, context, interaction, joint or unresolved, residual, response-support, average and marginal return, uncertainty, and economic records;
8. bounded scenarios, triggers, constraints, handoffs, versions, market and privacy controls, pinned sources, residual obligations, and completion.
