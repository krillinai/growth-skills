# Growth Learning System

Turn source artifacts into traceable, context-bounded learning units that can be found, challenged, refreshed, reused, corrected, and retired. Preserve original evidence and uncertainty; a summary, repeated claim, popular document, retrieved answer, or favorable result is not a verified lesson, decision, execution, or impact.

Read [learning-system-contract.md](references/learning-system-contract.md) before accepting sources, states, evidence, learning units, owners, contexts, taxonomies, versions, or uses. Read [evidence-synthesis-and-retrieval.md](references/evidence-synthesis-and-retrieval.md) before synthesizing, deduplicating, reconciling contradictions, comparing contexts, retrieving, ranking, or measuring reuse. Read [freshness-governance-and-actions.md](references/freshness-governance-and-actions.md) before assigning freshness, transfer, rights, privacy, correction, retirement, handoffs, or external actions. Read [output-contract.md](references/output-contract.md) before delivery. Use [playbook-sources.md](references/playbook-sources.md) for the pinned Growth Playbook basis.

## Select One Mode

| Mode | Use |
| --- | --- |
| `build` | Create a source registry, learning-unit system, taxonomy, retrieval contract, freshness rules, reuse measurement, and governance from supplied artifacts |
| `audit` | Inspect an existing library for provenance gaps, state conflation, winner bias, duplicates, contradictions, weak context, stale claims, poor retrieval, unsafe access, or vanity metrics |
| `refresh` | Version sources and learning units after evidence, definitions, contexts, rights, decisions, outcomes, corrections, consumers, or applicability change |

Name one mode and state `draft`, `evidence-pending`, `review-ready`, `published-record`, `review-required`, `stale`, `invalidated`, `superseded`, `retired`, or `expired`. State does not prove source rights, publication, comprehension, adoption, decision change, execution, causal impact, or success.

Route customer research, experiments, attribution, cohorts, metrics, data quality, competitive intelligence, campaigns, launches, incidents, legal review, and other source production to accountable specialists and owning Skills. Route one decision to `growth-decision-record`, recurring business review to `growth-operating-review`, portfolio risk to `growth-risk-management`, and retrospective causal learning to `growth-postmortem`. This Skill owns the cross-source registry, synthesis contract, retrieval, freshness, reuse, and retirement, not specialist work or execution.

## Freeze The System Contract

Record system ID and version, mode and state, decisions and intended uses, system and source owners, reviewers, publishers, correction and retirement owners, scope and exclusions, products and business models, customers and participant roles, markets and locales, source types, topics and mechanisms, metrics, periods, cutoff, maturity, access, rights, privacy, review and expiry, prior system, and external-action boundary.

Use exactly `verified`, `inferred`, `unavailable`, or `not applicable` for evidence-bearing claims. Keep source artifact, quote, observation, finding, claim, interpretation, insight, hypothesis, lesson, recommendation, decision, approval, authorization, action, verification, outcome, and causal estimate separate. Do not invent sources, provenance, private facts, evidence, consensus, confidence, rights, decisions, adoption, or impact.

## Inventory Sources And Lineage

For every artifact record stable source and family IDs, author or owner, publisher, date, version, original or derivative state, language, market, access, rights or supplied permission, confidentiality, method, population, period, metric definitions, maturity, exact claims, limitations, corrections, and downstream learning units.

Trace original, quoted, syndicated, translated, summarized, paraphrased, generated, and cited artifacts. Repetition, popularity, citations, and distribution reach are not independent corroboration. Do not copy restricted full sources when bounded metadata, citations, authorized excerpts, or links suffice.

## Build Bounded Learning Units

Record learning ID and version, exact claim, state, decision use, source claims and versions, evidence and counterevidence, assumptions, uncertainty, customer and participant roles, product and business model, market and locale, channel and system, metric and version, population, period, maturity, method, mechanism, necessary conditions, exclusions, applicability, prohibited transfer, limitations, owner, review, freshness, invalidation, expiry, consumers, and correction lineage.

Preserve positive, negative, flat, harmful, invalid, contradictory, and inconclusive evidence. Record eligible source population, included, excluded, unavailable, suppressed, and unreviewed coverage. Evidence quality and decision value do not depend on favorable direction.

## Synthesize Without Erasing Context

Separate observed pattern, proposed mechanism, transfer hypothesis, reusable principle, and local implementation. Keep incompatible contexts in linked views. Do not merge consumer and enterprise, marketplace sides, products, markets, metrics, methods, periods, or maturity merely because wording is similar.

Deduplicate derivative source families and repeated claims without discarding their reach or transformation history. Preserve definition, data, interpretation, mechanism, authority, and outcome conflicts in a contradiction ledger. Reconcile only with attributable scope and authority; otherwise leave conflict visible and affected units `review-required`.

## Retrieve For A Decision

Freeze the query's decision, user role, customer, product, market, metric, mechanism, horizon, cutoff, intended use, and access boundary. Filter and rank by applicability, evidence quality, maturity, freshness, contradiction, permission, and transfer distance, not clicks or citation count alone.

Return supporting, contradictory, negative, invalid, inconclusive, and unavailable evidence as applicable. Explain query, filters, ranking, source and learning versions, coverage, exclusions, conflicts, and no-result state. Retrieval does not prove a recommendation or decision.

## Govern Freshness And Transfer

Separate durable mechanisms from volatile benchmarks, prices, tactics, product workflows, platform capabilities, provider availability, policies, laws, and market conditions. Define scheduled review and event-driven invalidation for source, definition, metric, customer, product, market, platform, channel, price, policy, rights, evidence, incident, or outcome changes.

In `refresh`, preserve the ex-ante unit, evidence, uncertainty, dissent, applicability, and recommendation. Add new evidence, outcomes, corrections, changed definitions, and applicability by immutable version. Do not backdate knowledge, rewrite uncertainty, or equate favorable outcomes with sound learning. Mark consumers that must review, correct, or retire derived artifacts.

## Measure Reuse Without Vanity

Separate content supply, evidence coverage, discovery, retrieval, comprehension, citation, reuse, decision change, action, verification, mature outcome, and causal effect. Record learning version, consumer, decision record, exact changed field, date, evidence, rejection or nonuse, and limitation. Views and citations can show use, not value.

Do not add experiment uplifts or attribute later revenue to the library. Track prevented repetition, useful rejection, contradiction surfaced, stale guidance retired, and evidence gaps exposed. Use causal language only when a credible design supports it.

## External-Action Boundary

Create and read local artifacts only. Do not access authenticated document, wiki, drive, email, chat, analytics, CRM, experiment, product, support, HR, legal, or publishing systems; export or join personal data; ingest, publish, archive, delete, migrate, or change content, sources, permissions, taxonomies, records, training, decisions, or systems; contact or notify people; or claim ingestion, publication, adoption, decision change, execution, deletion, retirement, or impact without separate task-level authorization and controls.

## Completion Gate

Confirm the output passes [output-contract.md](references/output-contract.md); sources and derivative families are traceable; learning states remain distinct; no provenance, rights, or evidence was invented; unfavorable and missing evidence remains visible; duplicates do not inflate support; contexts and transfer are bounded; contradictions are not overwritten; retrieval is decision-specific and traceable; volatile claims expire; history is not rewritten; usage is not impact; sensitive data and source rights are protected; specialist work is routed; and no external action occurred.
