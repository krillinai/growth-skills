# Evidence Synthesis And Retrieval

## Synthesize In Layers

For every synthesis preserve:

1. exact source claims and evidence states;
2. compatible observations and definitions;
3. counterevidence, invalid, negative, flat, and inconclusive evidence;
4. interpretations and proposed mechanisms;
5. context similarities and differences;
6. transfer hypothesis and destination evidence needed;
7. bounded lesson, limitation, owner, review, and expiry.

Do not let a concise summary replace the source ledger.

## Deduplicate Without Losing Lineage

Group artifacts by original source and claim ancestry. Preserve transformations, languages, publishers, reach, and citation paths while counting independent support only once per genuinely independent evidence source. Record unresolved originals, circular citations, shared datasets, shared authors, reused participants, and common analytical inputs.

Similarity of wording is not proof of duplication; different wording is not proof of independence.

## Reconcile Contradictions

| Conflict | Preserve |
| --- | --- |
| Definition | Entity, formula, denominator, window, identity, source, and version |
| Data | Dataset, cutoff, population, maturity, revision, and quality state |
| Interpretation | Same evidence with different meaning, assumptions, and owner |
| Mechanism | Competing causal explanations and discriminating evidence |
| Context | Customer, product, market, channel, period, and operating differences |
| Authority | Different decision or publication rights by field and scope |
| Outcome revision | Ex-ante claim, later evidence, correction, and unresolved causality |

Build a contradiction ledger with exact claims, sources, overlap, compatible parts, governing authority if supplied, resolution, unresolved state, affected units, and downstream consumers. Do not select the newest, most senior, or most favorable answer by default.

## Compare Contexts Before Transfer

Record source and destination customer roles, problem, alternatives, product, model, market, locale, channel, platform, metric, population, method, period, maturity, incentives, economics, capacity, governance, and risks. Separate durable mechanism from local tactic or benchmark.

Use `directly applicable`, `adaptation required`, `hypothesis only`, `contradicted`, `insufficient evidence`, or `not applicable` only with recorded support. Require local evidence before performance, causal, legal, provider, or operating transfer.

## Define A Retrieval Contract

Record query ID and version, decision, user role, intended use, customer, product, market, metric, mechanism, horizon, as-of time, source and learning cutoff, permissions, required evidence, freshness, exclusions, and output form.

Rank using applicable context, evidence quality, source independence, maturity, freshness, contradiction, rights, and transfer distance. Popularity, views, citations, document age, or senior authorship can be displayed but cannot establish relevance or truth.

Return query terms, taxonomy expansion, filters, ranking factors, source and learning versions, results, counterevidence, incompatible views, exclusions, coverage, no-result state, conflicts, and next evidence. Never hide low coverage behind a confident answer.

## Measure Reuse As A Chain

| Layer | Evidence |
| --- | --- |
| Supply | Source and learning coverage, quality, freshness, and bias |
| Discovery | Query, retrieval, access, and result trace |
| Comprehension | User-confirmed interpretation or decision-ready restatement |
| Citation | Exact learning version referenced; not adoption |
| Reuse | Learning changes a design, analysis, alternative, guardrail, or question |
| Decision change | Decision record identifies the changed field and rationale |
| Action | Separately authorized and verified execution |
| Outcome | Mature customer and business result |
| Causal effect | Credible evidence that learning-system use changed the outcome |

Record useful rejection, nonuse, contradiction surfaced, repeated work prevented, stale advice retired, and evidence gaps exposed. Do not add experiment uplifts, count citations as independent value, or attribute all later results to the library.

## Refresh Without Hindsight

Keep ex-ante evidence, uncertainty, dissent, mechanism, applicability, recommendation, and cutoff immutable. Add ex-post evidence, changed definitions, outcomes, external events, corrections, consumer effects, and causal status by version.

Separate evidence quality, synthesis quality, decision quality, execution quality, outcome quality, and luck. A good outcome does not validate unsupported learning; a bad outcome does not erase a disciplined bounded lesson.
