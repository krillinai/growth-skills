# Freshness, Governance, And Actions

## Classify Freshness By Claim

| Claim type | Typical trigger |
| --- | --- |
| Durable mechanism | Contradictory evidence, changed necessary condition, or failed transfer |
| Metric or benchmark | Definition, population, source, maturity, market, or period change |
| Product workflow | Product, model, interface, entitlement, identity, or support change |
| Channel or platform tactic | Algorithm, policy, format, access, cost, saturation, or provider change |
| Price or economics | Offer, currency, cost, margin, cash, tax, discount, or cohort change |
| Market or governance | Customer, regulation, provider, rights, data, payment, invoice, or authority change |

Record last verified date, evidence cutoff, review cadence, event triggers, owner, current state, expiry, and replacement. Lack of new evidence does not silently renew a claim.

## Correct And Retire Responsibly

Preserve original and corrected values, source, author, reason, date, affected claims, learning units, queries, decisions, consumers, publications, and required downstream action. Use `review-required`, `stale`, `invalidated`, `superseded`, `retired`, or `expired` as supported.

Retirement stops new guidance; it does not delete source lineage, prior decisions, rights, retention, correction, or audit obligations. Require independent proof before claiming downstream correction or deletion.

## Govern Rights And Attribution

Record author, publisher, owner, license or supplied permission, allowed use, quotation or excerpt boundary, confidentiality, access class, publication rights, attribution, consent, withdrawal, retention, deletion, and audit. Internal access does not imply public publication rights.

Prefer links, metadata, citations, and minimum authorized excerpts over copying entire paywalled, copyrighted, private, customer, employee, or vendor sources. Route unresolved rights and legal conclusions to accountable specialists; do not claim clearance.

## Protect Personal And Sensitive Data

Use minimum-necessary aggregate, redacted, pseudonymous, access-controlled, and small-cell-suppressed evidence. Use accountable roles rather than names unless identity is necessary, proportionate, and authorized.

Do not store credentials, payment details, private messages, health or precise financial information, protected traits, precise location, compensation, employee reviews, raw ratings, or full histories by default. Never expose secrets. Do not rank named people for consequential pricing, service, credit, health, employment, bonuses, promotion, discipline, or termination.

Record purpose, authority or supplied basis, consent, fields, population, access, sharing, residency, retention, deletion, correction, withdrawal, incident, and audit. Review accessibility, fairness, proxy discrimination, retaliation, and misuse.

## Transfer Across Markets Carefully

Respond in the user's requested language. For Simplified Chinese, write native Simplified Chinese while preserving exact source names, IDs, versions, formulas, quotations, and required record states where precision matters.

Treat market, language, locale, currency, customer, product, channel, platform, app distribution, identity, payment, invoice, data, content, support, regulation, source rights, metrics, owners, and decision authority separately. Revalidate local evidence, definitions, context, mechanisms, freshness, access, rights, applicability, exclusions, correction, and retirement. Use current direct sources and locally accountable expertise. Avoid legal, tax, regulatory, provider, permission, authorization, and performance conclusions.

## Maintain Specialist Handoffs

Record exact question, decision changed, scope, customer, product, market, source cutoff, prohibited inference, accountable specialist, owner, expected artifact and version, date, evidence state, limitations, conflicts, return status, expiry, and learning units affected. Use `unrequested`, `requested`, `acknowledged`, `blocked`, `returned`, `rejected`, `superseded`, or `expired`.

Do not replace source owners or claim routed analysis, review, correction, remediation, or decision occurred.

## Specify Approval-Ready External Actions

For each proposed ingestion, migration, permission, taxonomy, publication, notification, correction, archive, deletion, training, or integration record exact system, object, scope, population, environment, source and learning versions, rights, privacy, owner, reviewers, approvers, authorizer, executor, access, dependencies, retention, legal hold, withdrawal, dry run, staged rollout, monitoring, stop, rollback, correction, audit, verification, and expiry.

Create local specifications and requests only. Do not access authenticated systems; export or join data; ingest, publish, migrate, archive, delete, or change sources, content, permissions, taxonomies, records, training, or systems; contact or notify people; or claim deployment, correction, deletion, adoption, or impact without separate task-level authorization and controls.

## Record Completion Honestly

State local artifacts created or read, sources and versions used, evidence unavailable, rights unresolved, learning units created or changed, conflicts preserved, handoffs proposed, external actions proposed, actions separately authorized, actions actually performed, verification observed, consumers affected, and next owner. If no external action occurred, say so.
