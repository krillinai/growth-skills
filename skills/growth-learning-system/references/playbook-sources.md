## Source Rules

- Cite exact pinned sections and preserve source versions.
- Keep Playbook concepts separate from direct organization evidence.
- Record customer, product, market, method, metric, period, maturity, limitation, and transfer boundary.
- Do not turn a framework, case, expert statement, citation, or repeated claim into causal proof or universal practice.
- Use current direct sources for volatile product, platform, market, price, rights, legal, regulatory, and provider claims.
- Record a newer Playbook commit explicitly rather than silently mixing versions.
