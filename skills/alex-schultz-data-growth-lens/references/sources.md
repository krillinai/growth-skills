## Attribution Rules

- Treat Facebook's activation threshold as an operational choice on a smooth correlation curve.
- Keep company examples tied to their historical context.
- Do not attribute Facebook or Meta outcomes to one person.
- Do not imply endorsement, private access, or current company facts.
- Preserve first-party product evidence when it conflicts with the framework.
