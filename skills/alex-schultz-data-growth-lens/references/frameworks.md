# Framework Reference

## Retention Foundation

Plot cohorts at the product's natural interval and segment by source, market, use case, device, activation path, and product state. Look for a stable retained group and investigate who remains and why. Acquisition amplifies the curve; it does not repair it.

## Growth Accounting

```text
net growth = new users + resurrected users - churned users
```

Use consistent active, churn, and resurrection definitions. Compare absolute counts and rates by segment. The same net result can require acquisition, retention, or resurrection work.

## Magic Moment Discovery

Interview retained users about first value, propose candidate behaviors, then measure their relationship with later retention. Test whether interventions that increase the behavior also improve retention. The behavior is a proxy until causal evidence strengthens it.

## Behavior-Curve Validation

Plot behavior intensity against retained outcomes. Select an operational threshold that balances reach, predictive value, clarity, and controllability. Preserve the continuous curve and uncertainty; do not invent a natural cliff.

## One Company Goal

Choose a top-level metric representing delivered value and add input and guardrail metrics. Use it to decentralize decisions, not to erase revenue, trust, quality, safety, or ecosystem tradeoffs.

## Data-Debt Audit

Map critical journey events, properties, identity, failure states, exposure logging, metric definitions, lineage, latency, and quality checks. Prioritize gaps that block a current decision. Instrumentation is growth infrastructure, not reporting cleanup.

## Marginal-User Messaging

Identify users near the next value event rather than optimizing for highly engaged users. Define trigger, relevance, timing, channel, frequency, control, and fatigue guardrails. Measure downstream value and retention, not opens alone.

## Acquisition Sequence

Evaluate in order: channel, targeting, creative, conversion. Confirm audience access and channel-product fit before polishing creative or downstream conversion. Measure marginal retained quality and saturation.

## Structural Barrier Removal

For language, geography, payments, devices, or connectivity, confirm existing value, size the blocked retained demand, localize the whole journey, and measure local activation and retention. Translation alone is not a growth strategy.

## Payload-Frequency-Conversion Map

```text
viral impact = payload x frequency x recipient conversion
```

Add recipient value, retained quality, cycle time, incrementality, exposure fatigue, cost, and abuse. Treat the equation as a mechanism map, not a forecast.
